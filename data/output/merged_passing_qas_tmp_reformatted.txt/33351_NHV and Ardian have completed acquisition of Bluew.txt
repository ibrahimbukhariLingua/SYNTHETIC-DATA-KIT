NHV and Ardian have completed acquisition of Blueway


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } NHV, an international helicopter transportation company, on 22 December announced that it had completed the acquisition of Blueway, a specialist in helicopter rental. NHV, supported by Ardian, its majority shareholder, and its chairman and founder Eric van Hal, have also ordered 16 Airbus EC175 helicopters. The new group is the only one present in all countries which produce natural gas and oil in the North Sea, and also has a leading position in West Africa. The new entity now employs about 650 people worldwide, and has combined turnover of USD270m, a statement says.
