Edmond de Rothschild recruits a head for Germany and Austria


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Since the beginning of August, Maximilian Wolters has been serving as head for the German and Austrian wealth management markets in the private merchant banking division of Edmond de Rothschild. Wolters had previously served as head of single offices at Sal. Oppenheim. According to Das Investment, he will be based in Frankfurt, and will report to Konstantin Mettenheimer, chairman of the group for Germany.
