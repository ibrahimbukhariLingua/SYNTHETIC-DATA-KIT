Syz manager Eric Bendahan launches a boutique


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The former manager and head of European equities at Syz Asset Management, Eric Bendahan, who has been replaced in that role by Michael Clements (see elsewhere in Newsmanagers), is preparing to launch his own boutique and funds during fourth quarter 2014, Reuters reports. In an announcement made on Tuesday, 16 September, Bendahan stated that he is in the process of creating a new firm, entitled Eleva Capital, based in London. He also plans to launch a pan-European long-only equity fund, for which he is awating approval from the regulatory authorities. The vehicle is expected to be domiciled in Luxembourg.
