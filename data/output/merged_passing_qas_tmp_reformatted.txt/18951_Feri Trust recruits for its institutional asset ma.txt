Feri Trust recruits for its institutional asset management unit in Germany


Markus Fila, client relationship manager for Germany and Austria at Aviva Investors in Frankfurt, and previously institutional sales manager at Invesco Austria, is joining the German institutional client team at Feri Trust (MLP group). He will be responsible for developing business relationships with companies and pension funds, to which he will offer multi-asset class solutions, private equity, hedge funds, real estate investments, and overlay risk management.
