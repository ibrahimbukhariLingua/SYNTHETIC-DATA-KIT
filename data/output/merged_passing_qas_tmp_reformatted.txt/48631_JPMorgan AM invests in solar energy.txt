JPMorgan AM invests in solar energy


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; }a:link { } JPMorgan Asset Management, via its affiliate Global Real Assets, has founded a joint venture with Sonnedix to invest in solar energy. The joint venture, entitled Sonnedix Power Holdings, currently manages 17 energy production stations located in France, Italy, Spain, Thailand, and Puerto Rico. Its portfolio also inclues 18 stations under construction, including sites in Chile, the United Kingdom and South Africa. The joint venture anticipates investment of over EUR300m.
