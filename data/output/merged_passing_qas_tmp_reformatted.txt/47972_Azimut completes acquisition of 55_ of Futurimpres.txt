Azimut completes acquisition of 55% of Futurimpresa


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The Italian asset management firm Azimut has completed its acquisition of 55% of Futurimpresa, a company specialised in private equity operations created by the chambers of commerce of Milan, Bergamo, Brescia and Como. The operation, announced on 6 August last year, aims to build a partnership to develop entrepreneurial synergies in the investment sector for small and mid-sized businesses, with the objective of supporting the region where they operate, a statement says. The acquisition was performed through a reserved capital increased totalling about EUR2.5bn.
