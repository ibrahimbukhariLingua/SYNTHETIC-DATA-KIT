@page { margin: 0.79in }p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 115% }Amundihas recruited Gloria Yu from Baring Asset Management (Barings) assenior manager, responsible for fund distribution in Hong Kong andChina, Asian Investor reports.
