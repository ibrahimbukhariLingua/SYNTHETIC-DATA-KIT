Capricorn appoints partner


The US asset management firm Capricorn Investment Group has recruited Michaela Edwards as a partner at its New York office. Edwards had previously worked at Norges Bank Investment Management, where she spent nine years as a senior portfolio manager for the Norwegian sovereign fund. Edwards was responsible for sourcing, constructing and managing an international environmental portfolio with USD2bn in assets, and oversaw all of the mandates for Norges Bank IM in Latin America and developed markets. Capricorn specialises in sustainable finance and impact investment via its multi-asset portfolios. The company, founded in 2001 and based in Palo Alto, has over USD5bn in assets under management.
