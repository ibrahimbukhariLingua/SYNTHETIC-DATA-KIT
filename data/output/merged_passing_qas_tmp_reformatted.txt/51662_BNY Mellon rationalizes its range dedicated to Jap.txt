BNY Mellon rationalizes its range dedicated to Japan


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } According to Citywire, BNY Mellon is going to rationalize its range of Japan funds, whose funds are managed by Kazuya Kurosawa. The manager will provide a way for clients invested in the smallest of the funds (BNY Mellon Japan Equity Small Cap Opportunities, USD8m in assets) to transfer their assets for free to the BNY Mellon Japan Equity Small Cap Focus fund, which has USD32m in assets. The two funds will be merged on 23 August.
