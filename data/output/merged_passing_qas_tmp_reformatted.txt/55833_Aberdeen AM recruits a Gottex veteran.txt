Aberdeen AM recruits a Gottex veteran


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Aberdeen Asset Management has recruited James Hughes to the newly-created position of head of insurance solutions as part of its investment solutions activity. Hughes joins from Gottex Asset Management (UK) Limited, where he had been managing director, multi-asset class solutions. Before that, he worked for more than eight years at HSBC as chief investment officer at HSBC Insurance, responsible for assets totalling over USD95bn in 14 countries. Hughes will report to Archie Struthers, global head of investment solutions. Aberdeen has over GBP120bn in assets under management on behalf of insurance clients (as of 30 September 2015).
