Carlyle promotes a head of activities in Australia and New Zealand


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Carlyle Group on 16 June announced that it has promoted David Bluff to managing director and head of its buyout team in Australia and New Zealand. Bluff is based in Sydney, and had previously served as director of the team. Bluff joined Carlyle in 2008 and has 15 years of experience in private equity and corporate financing in Australia, Europe and the United States. Before joining Carlyle, Bluff worked at SABMiller as head of acquisitions for Europe and the Americas. Before that, he served at JP Morgan on the team in charge of mergers and acquisitions in Australia and the United Kingdom.
