Columbia Threadneedle recruits a salesperson in Italy


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Columbia Threadneedle Investments has recruited Aldo Melloni for its Italian sales team, as a sales manager. Melloni joins from the Allianz group, where he spent 11 years. For the past four years, he had been key account manager at Allianz GI. At Columbia Threadneedle, Melloni will be responsible for reinforcing the activities of the company serving the retail, family office and private banking segments. The British asset management firm has been present in Italy since 2008.
