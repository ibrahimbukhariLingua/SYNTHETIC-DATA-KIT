New York Life IM acquires IndexIQ


New York Life Investment Mangement (NYLIM), the third-party asset management affiliate of the US insurer New York Life Insurance, has made a high-profile debut in the ETF sector, with the acquisition of IndexIQ, a company specialised in liquid index-based alternative funds. The acquisition price has not been disclosed.After the operation, which is expected to be completed in first half 2015, IndexIQ will be completely integrated into NYLIM and its product ranges will be available from the MainStay Investments platform, an affiliate of New York Life dedicated to distribution of mutual funds, which has USD101bn in assets under management. MainStay Investments will see a significant increase in its assets, as it will take on USD1.5bn in assets managed by IndexIQ in a range of 12 funds.
