Siparex sells stake in Bernadet


In a joint statement, the private equity firm Siparex and the Amundi group announced the sale of a stake in the Bernadet group by Siparex to Laurent Bernadet, who is teaming up for the occasion with Amundi Private Equity Funds (Amundi PEF). Siparex joined the capital in 2007. The Bernadet Group is a specialist in the professions of large projects, civil engineering, the cement industry and the environment. The group has 370 employees and consolidated earnings of EUR70m in South-Western France. A statement says that “in association with Amundi PEF, Laurent Bernadet plans to continue to develop the group through targeted acquisitions to make it possible to continue diversification of activities and to extend territorial coverage.”
