AllianzGI appoints a head of institutionals for United Kingdom


Allianz Global Investors (AllianzGI) has appointed Margaret Frost as head of institutional UK, effective October 1st, 2016.Previously, she headed the Generalist Portfolio Management team for Rogge Global Partners (RGP), the London-based global fixed income specialist acquired by AllianzGI in June of this year. As well as holding this position from 2011, Margaret has led the firm’s marketing and client relationships since 2015.In her new role, Margaret Frost will lead AllianzGI’s efforts across sales, consultant relations and client account management in the UK, reporting to Irshaad Ahmad, head of institutional Europe. In addition, she will also be responsible for coordinating AllianzGI’s global consultant relations activities.
