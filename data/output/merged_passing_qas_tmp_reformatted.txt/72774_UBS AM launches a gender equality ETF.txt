UBS AM launches a gender equality ETF


UBS Asset Management (UBS AM) is adding to its range of ETFs with the launch of a fund focused on gender equality, the specialist foundation Equileap announced on 8 January. The new vehicle, UBS (Irl) ETF plc â€“ Global Gender Equality Ucits ETF, is part of a range of impact investment products. The ETF will invest in the Solactive Equileap Global Gender Equality 100 Leaders index, which tracks the 100 largest global companies with a solid balance sheet in gender diversity and sustainable development. The fund, listed on the Swiss Exchange SIX on 8 January, is the product of a collaboration between UBS AM and UBS Wealth Management. The asset management firm has decided to dedicate 5% of the management fees from this ETF to philanthropic projects.
