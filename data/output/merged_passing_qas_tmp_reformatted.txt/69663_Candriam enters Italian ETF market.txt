Candriam enters Italian ETF market


Candriam Investors Group is entering the Italian ETF market, Bluerating reports. Since 9 May, five ESG smart beta ETFs from Candriam Luxembourg have been launched on the Milan stock exchange. These can be used to invest in equity and bond markets.
