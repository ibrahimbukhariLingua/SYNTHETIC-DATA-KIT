ATP and PensionDanmark acquire Marriott hotel in Copenhagen


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The Danish pension fund ATP Ejendomme and PensionDanmark have acquired the hotel complex which is home to the Marriott Hotel in Copenhagen for DKK1bn, Ejendom reports. The two investors will co-own the property 50/50. The property is leased to BC Hospitality Group. The vendor is Solstra Investments, which controls BC Hospitality Group.
