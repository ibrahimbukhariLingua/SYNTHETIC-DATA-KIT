Pimco adds to equity management unit


Pimco has recruited two new people as additions to its equity management unit, led by Virginie Maisonneuve. The US asset management firm on 18 December announced the appointment of Giles Money as senior vice president and manager, specialised in growth equity strategies. He joins Pimco from Schroders Investment Management, where he was a manager specialised in utilities.Lucrecia Tam has been appointed as senior vice president and equity analyst specialised in industrial stocks. She also joins from Schroders Investment Management.The two new recruits will be based in London.
