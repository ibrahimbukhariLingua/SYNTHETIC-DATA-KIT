SSGA signs partnership with China’s E Fund Management


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The Chinese asset management firm E Fund Management and State Street Global Advisors (SSGA), the asset management division of State Street Corporation, have announced that they have signed an agreement to explore strategic possibilities for development on international markets, including the asset management market in China. SSGA claims a place as the world’s number three asset management firm, with assets of USD2.4trn as of the end of 2016, while E Fund Management is number three on its domestic market, with about USD140bn in assets as of the end of December 2016. The partnership will aim to facilitate information exchange about development of products and innovation and to strengthen the ability of both firms to gain market share in local and international markets.
