World - Pension funds plan to increase exposure to equities


According to the most recent asset allocation survey of 60 pension funds (52% based in Europe and 48% in North America) by bfinance, 46% of managers interviewed in March were planning to increase their allocation to equities in the next 12 months, compared with 19% five months previously. In these five months, 44% of funds increased their exposure to bonds. In the next twelve months, 25% are planning to increase their allocations to bonds, while 42% are planning to reduce it. Five months ago, the corresponding proportions were 30% and 11%, respectively.
