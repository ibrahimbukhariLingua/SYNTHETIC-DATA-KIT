China AM forges a strategic alliance with US firm PanAgora


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The Chinese asset management firm China Asset Management (China AM) has formed a strategic alliance with PanAgora Asset Management, an asset management firm based in Boston, to developed risk parity multi asset class products, the specialist website Asian Investor reports. The partnership may extend to other short-term investment strategies, the Asian website states. China AM will provide the management of these new strategies, while PanAgora will provide all research expertise. The new products will invest in Chinese assets, and will be sold to clients based in continental China.
