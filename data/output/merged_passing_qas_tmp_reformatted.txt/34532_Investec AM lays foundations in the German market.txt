Investec AM lays foundations in the German market


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Investec Asset Management is shifting into high gear on the German market. The British asset management firm on Monday, 3 November opened an office in Frankfurt, and announced the appointment of Aymeric François as director of sales in charge of sales of funds to wholesale clients, a newly-created position. Before joining Investec AM, François was executive director for sales of asset management at Goldman Sachs, targeting private banks, funds of funds, and family offices in Germany. François will wotk at the Frankfurt office, in close collaboration with Michael Steiner, head of distribution to institutional clients. In a statement, Stef Bogaars, director of sales for European clients at Investec AM, emphasized the importance of the German market for the development of the asset management firm.
