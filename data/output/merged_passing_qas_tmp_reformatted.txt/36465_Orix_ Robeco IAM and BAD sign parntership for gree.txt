Orix, Robeco IAM and BAD sign parntership for green private equity


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The Asian Development Bank (ADB), Orix Corporation and Robeco Institutional Asset Management B.V. (Robeco) have announced the creation of Asia Climate Partners (ACP), a joint venture which will invest in private equity as part of green projects throughout the Asian region. The new firm, ACP, which has seed capital of USD400m, will be based in Hong Kong, and led by a team of private equity professionals which will prefer investments in renewable energeies, clean technologies, energy efficiency, water, agriculture, forestry, and other environmentally friendly projects.
