Wells Fargo AM plans to launch first ETF


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The fund management affiliate of Wells Fargo & Co, Wells Fargo Asset Management, is planning to launch its first ETF in the upcoming three to six months, Bloomberg reports, citing directors. The firm is studying a multi-factor ETF, an improved version of the smart beta ETF. It will rely on the resources of Analytic Investors, a quantitative asset management firm which it has recently acquired.
