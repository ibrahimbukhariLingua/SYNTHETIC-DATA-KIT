Axiom Asia Private Capital completes a fund with USD1bn


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The investment company Axiom Asia Private Capital, based in Singapore, has closed its fourth fund, Axiom Axia IV, with a total sum in engagements of USD1.03bn. The fund has thus exceeded its initial objective of USD750m. Like its predecessors, the new vehicle will invest in Asian venture capital, private equity and equity growth funds.
