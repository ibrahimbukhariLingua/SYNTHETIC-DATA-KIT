J.P. Morgan AM adds to analyst teams


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } J.P. Morgan Asset Management on 29 September announced that it has recruited David Stubbs to the position of global market strategist on its Global Markets Insights Strategy team. In his new role, he will be responsible for providing economic and market analysis to financial advisers and institutional investors, concentrating on the United Kingdom and Europe. He will be based in London, and will report to Stephanit Flanders, chief market strategist for the United Kingdom and Europe. Before joining the US asset management firm, Stubbs had been senior macro strategist at MRB Partners in New York. He previously served in a similar role at Heitman Securities in London, and at the United Nations office for economic and social affairs.
