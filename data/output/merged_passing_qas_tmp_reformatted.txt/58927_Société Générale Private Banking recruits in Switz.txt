Société Générale Private Banking recruits in Switzerland


Société Générale Private Banking Schweiz (SGBPS) is adding to its presence in German-speaking Switzerland, with the appointments of Cédrick Merour andMartin Petermann, who will both be based in Zurich. Merour, previously head of market solutions at SGPBS in Geneva, has been appointed as head of the financial intermediaries (FIM) segmnt for the Zurich region. He will now report to Tobias Wagner, head of the Zurich office, and David Saliné, head of the FIM segment for Switzerland.Martin Petermann, for his part, joined SGPBS in August this year as senior head of clients. He previously worked at Deutsche Bank, UBS and BNP Paribas.
