Nuveen Investments plants a foothold in the ETF market


The US asset management firm Nuveen Investments, an affiliate of the TIAA-CREF group, has recruited Martin Kremenstein as managing director and head of ETFs. He will be based in New York and will report to Greg Bottjer, managing director and head of product strategy, and will be responsible for accelerating the development of the ETF activity at the asset management firm.Kremenstein, who has over 20 years of experience, previously worked as managing director of the asset & wealth management division of Deutsche Bank, where he had been responsible for ETF activities in the United States.
