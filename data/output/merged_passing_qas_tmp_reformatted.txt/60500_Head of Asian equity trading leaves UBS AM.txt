Head of Asian equity trading leaves UBS AM


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Jimmy Chua, previously head of Asian equity trading at UBS Asset Management in Singapore, will be leaving the asset management firm, Asian Investor reports. The departure comes a few months after the arrival of Martin David in Singapore as head of trading for Asia, covering both equities and bonds. David started in the role in December 2016, the Asian news website reports. The departure date for Chua has not been disclosed.
