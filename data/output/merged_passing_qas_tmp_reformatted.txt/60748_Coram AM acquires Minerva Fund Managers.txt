Coram AM acquires Minerva Fund Managers


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The British asset management firm Coram Asset Management (Coram AM), an affiliate of the MitonOptimal group, has announced the acquisition of Minerva Fund Managers, a British firm specialised in the management of discretionary funds. The transaction remains subject to approval by the regulatory authorities, Coram AM states. “The operation makes it possible to combine two experienced investment teams, and will allow Coram to add to its range of portfolio modeling services, now including expertise in socially responsible investment,” Coram AM says in a statement. Minerva Fund Managers was founded in 1990. Since its inception, it has notably developed a range of SRI funds.
