Two Danish pension funds acquire 50% of an offshore wind farm


The Danish pension funds PensionDanmark and PKA have joined forces to acquire 50% of an offshore wind farm (30% from the former and 20% from PKA), from the public company DONG Energy A/S, for a total of about DEK6bn, or about EUR804.6m, Responsible Investor reports.
