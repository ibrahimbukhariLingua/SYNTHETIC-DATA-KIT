Fidelity launches shares in Asia High Yield Fund hedged for currency risks


p { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } Fidelity International announced on 3 February that since 10 January, shares in the Fidelity Asian High Yield Fund, managed by Bryan Collins, have been available in euros hedged for currency risks. This allows investors in Asian high yield bonds to invest in the product without facing the consequences of a choice of currency, says Andreas Schmid, director of distribution to private banks and wealth managers at Fidelity Germany.The new share classes for the fund, whose benchmark currency is the euro, are Fidelity Asian High Yield A-EUR (hedged), with the ISIN code LU0575482749, and Fidelity Asian High Yield Y-EUR (hedged), with the ISIN code LU0575482582.
