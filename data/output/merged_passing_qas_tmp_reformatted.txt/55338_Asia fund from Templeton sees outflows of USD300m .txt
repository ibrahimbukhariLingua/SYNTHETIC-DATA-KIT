Asia fund from Templeton sees outflows of USD300m in January


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The Templeton Asia Growth fund saw outflows of nearly USD300m last month, the Wall Street Journal reports. Outflows are thus accelerating for the fund, which had for years been the largest of its kind in Asia. The outflows are equivalent to more than 5% of its assets of USD5bn as of the beginning of the month.
