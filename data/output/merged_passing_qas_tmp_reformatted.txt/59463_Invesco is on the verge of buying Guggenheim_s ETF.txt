Invesco is on the verge of buying Guggenheim’s ETF business


Invesco is nearing an agreement to buy Guggenheim Partners’s ETF business for more than USD1 billion, people familiar with the matter told The Wall Street Journal.The proposed deal would swap the ETF platform for cash, a portion of which would be deferred until performance targets are met, one person said. The Guggenheim ETF business manages UD36 billion in assets, the person said, according to the WSJ
