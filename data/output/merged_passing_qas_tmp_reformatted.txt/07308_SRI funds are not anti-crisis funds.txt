SRI funds are not anti-crisis funds


p { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } In an interview with La Tribune, Noël Amenc says that according to a study by the Edhec Risk Institute, in the period 2007-2009, SRI funds did not outperform traditional funds during the crisis. “Stock-picking is not an effective method of risk management, and does not protect investors against extreme losses,” he notes, “and talking about SRI as a solution or a clear direction after the crisis is a more ideological than genuinely scientific posture.”
