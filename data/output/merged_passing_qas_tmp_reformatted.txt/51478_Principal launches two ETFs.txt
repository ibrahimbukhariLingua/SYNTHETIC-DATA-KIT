Principal launches two ETFs


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The asset management firm Principal, based in the United States, has announced that it has listed two smart beta ETFs on Nasdaq, the Principal Healthcare Innovators Index ETF and the Principal Millenials Index ETF. The first of these, which replicates the Nasdaq US Healthcare Innovators Index, offers exposure to young firms specialised in health (biotech, life sciences). The second, which replicates the Nasdaq Global Millenial Opportunity fund, offers exposure to companies specialised in social networks, digital media, Technologies, lifestyle, travel and leisure. The two new products are managed by Jeff Schwarte, Mark Nebelung and Paul Kim. Assets under management at the Principal group total over USD572bn, of which USD402n are managed by Principal Global Investors.
