BlackRock launches an electric vehicles ETF


BlackRock, the largest asset management provider worldwide, has announced the launch of an ETF dedicated to electric vehicles and automotive technologies, via its affiliate iShares. The ETF, iShares Electric Vehicle and Driving Technology UCITS, aims to “benefit from the significant growth in sustainable energy sources for transportation worldwide, at a time when investors are seeking to build portfolios which better reflect their personal opinions in environmental, social and governance (ESG) considerations,” the firm explains in a statement. The new tracker fund comes as an addition to a range of 16 themed ETFs and 27 ESG ETFs. In total, iShares has over 800 ETFs under management, with $1.7trn in assets under management as of 31 December 2018.
