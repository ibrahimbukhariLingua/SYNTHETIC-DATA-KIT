Madison International Realty sells the Trianon tower in Frankfurt


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The New York-based Madison International Realty group has announced the sale of the famous Trianon office tower in Frankfurt to Northstar Realty Finance Corp for about EUR540m. Madison International Raalty was co-owner of the Trianon tower with Eurozone Office Fund, a fund from Morgan Stanley.
