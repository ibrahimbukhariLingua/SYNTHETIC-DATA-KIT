PGGM working on impact measurement metrics


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The Netherlands-based asset management firm PGGM (EUR200bn in assets) will be working with Sinzer, a local provider of social impact management metrics, to strengthen its understanding of the social impact of its portfolio, IPE.com reports. PGGM will be studying new metrics to measure impact, including reducing carbon footprint and water efficiency through new technologies.
