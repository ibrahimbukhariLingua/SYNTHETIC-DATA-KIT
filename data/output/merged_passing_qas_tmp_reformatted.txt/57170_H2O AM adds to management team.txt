H2O AM adds to management team


H2O AM, led by Bruno Crastes, has recently recruited Romain Mercier as portfolio manager in London. Mercier officially joined the asset management firm in July, according to his LinkedIn profile. Mercier joins from Amundi in London, where he had worked for slightly over 12 years in various positions, including deputy head of risk mamangement, and absolute return portfolio manager. According to Citywire Selector, which broke the news, Mercier will work alongside Pascal Dubreuil, also formerly of Amundi, to manage the H2O MultiAggregate fund.
