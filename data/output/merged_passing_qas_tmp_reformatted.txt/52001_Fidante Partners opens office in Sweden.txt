Fidante Partners opens office in Sweden


Fidante Partners Europe (ex Dexion Capital) has opened an office in Stockholm, Sweden, in response to growing demand for alternative investments from pension funds and other institutional investors in the region, InvestmentEurope reports. The asset manager also announced the appointment of Christian Andersson to lead business growth in the region. He has had 17 years of experience developing investment and risk solutions for Nordic pension funds and life insurance companies.
