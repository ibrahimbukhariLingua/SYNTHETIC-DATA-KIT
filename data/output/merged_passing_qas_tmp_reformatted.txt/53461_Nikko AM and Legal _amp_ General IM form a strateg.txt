Nikko AM and Legal &amp; General IM form a strategic alliance


The Japanese asset management firm Nikko Asset Management (Nikko AM) and its British counterpart Legal & General Investment Management (LGIM) have announced that they have signed a cooperation agreement covering the provision of portfolio management services. Under the agreement, LGIM and Legal & General Investment Management America will provide global bond products, which Nikko AM will distribute to Japanese investors, largely Japanese banks and insurance companies. The first funds concerned will be launched in mid-2016, the partners say. LGIM has also agreed to facilitate marketing and sales of products from Nikko AM in the United Kingdom and other countries.
