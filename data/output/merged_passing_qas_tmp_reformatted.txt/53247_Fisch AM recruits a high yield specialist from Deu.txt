Fisch AM recruits a high yield specialist from Deutsche AM


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Fisch Asset Management has announced the recruitment of Kyle Kloc as manager of its FISCH Bond Global High Yield and FISCH Global Corporate funds. He will also be responsible for research on European and US issuers. Kloc joined the Swiss boutique in early May. He previously worked at Deutsche Asset Management, where he had been co-director of European and global high yield strategies, from 2007 to 2015, a statement says.
