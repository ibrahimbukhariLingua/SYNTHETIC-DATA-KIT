Banca Ifigest recruits a manager


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Banca Ifigest, an independent private bank active in wealth management for private and institutional clients, has recruited Andrea Scauri in Milan. Scauri will manage a sub-fund of the Luxembourg Sicav from Pharus Management, for which Banca Ifigest has obtained a management mandate, and which will invest in equities from southern Europe. The arrival of Scauri comes as part of a growth and reinforcement plan for Banca Ifigest, which aims to become a “banking boutique” of reference in asset management. Scauri had previously worked at Mediobanca, where he was most recently director of the industrial team, covering oil and gas companies and the household consumer goods sector.
