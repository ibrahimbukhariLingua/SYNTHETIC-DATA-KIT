BP group called on to better manage risks


p { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } A group of investors led by Christian Brothers Investment Services (CBIS) is planning to propose a resolution in January that would have the oil group BP pledge to improve its risk management, SocialFunds reports. In an interim declaration published with the draft resolution, the group of investors invites the board at BP to undertake an evaluation of its current risks and plans to manage risks for its activities in North America, and to release the results to shareholders.
