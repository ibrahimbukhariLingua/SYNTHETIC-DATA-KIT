Outflows from bond funds continue in September


UK bond funds have continued to experience redemptions, with outflows of GBP515m in September, the largest since June 2013, according to the latest statistics from the British Investment Assocation.For third quarter overall, bond funds had outflows of GBP891m, after redemptions totalling only GBP96m in second quarter, and inflows of GBP379m in first quarter.Funds dedicated to equities, however, were most popular in September, for a fifth consecutive month of net inflows of GBP1.1bn. In the asset class, however, one exception is funds dedicated to Asian equities, which completed September with net inflows of GBP129m.In all asset classes combined, retail inflows in September totalled GBP1.1bn, compared with GBP825m one year earlier.
