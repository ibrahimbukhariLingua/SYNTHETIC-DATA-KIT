State Street GA recruits a manager from ETF Securities


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The US asset management firm State Street Global Advisors (SSGA) has recruited Philippe Roset to the position of country head for the Netherlands at SPDR ETF, its ETF dedicated affiliate. The new recruit will be based in Amsterdam, and will report to Alexis Marinof, head of SPDR ETF for Europe, the Middle East and Africa. Roset joins SSGA from ETF Securities, where he had served as head for Benelux for over three years.
