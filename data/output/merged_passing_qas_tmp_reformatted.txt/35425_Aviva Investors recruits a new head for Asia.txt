Aviva Investors recruits a new head for Asia


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Aviva Investors has appointed Kevin Talbot as chief executive officer for Asia Pacific, while retaining his current responsibilities as chief investment officer for Asia. He replaces Tahnoon Pasha, who left the asset management firm in May this year. Talbot, who will be based in Singapore, joined Aviva Investors in 2010 as chief investment officer for fixed income for Asia. He previously worked at ANZ as director for private banking. Before that, he was chief investment officer for listed assets for Asia at AMP Capital Investors.
