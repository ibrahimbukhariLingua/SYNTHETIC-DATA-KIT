Moody’s publishes a new methodology for project finance ratings


p { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } The ratings agency Moody’s on 21 December published a methodology for project finance (“Generic Project Finance Methodology”), in order to improve the transparency and logic of its approach to rating issuers in this sector, which is not covered by existing methodologies. The new global methodology concerns projects such as stadiums, parking garages, and industrial installations, which are not included in the current ratings methodologies, Moody’s explains.
