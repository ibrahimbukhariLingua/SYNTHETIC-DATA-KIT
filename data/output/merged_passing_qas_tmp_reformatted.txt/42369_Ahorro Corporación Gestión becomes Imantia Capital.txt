Ahorro Corporación Gestión becomes Imantia Capital


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } In the wake of the acquisition of the firm by Abanca, the asset management firm Ahorro Corporación Gestión is changing names, to become Imantia Capital, the Spanish specialist press reports. The entirely restructured asset management firm started its activtiies with EUR3bn in assets under management, 39% of which came from Abanca, and 52% from Ahorro Corporación.
