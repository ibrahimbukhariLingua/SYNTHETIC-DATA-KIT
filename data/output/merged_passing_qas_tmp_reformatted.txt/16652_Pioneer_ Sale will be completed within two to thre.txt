Pioneer: Sale will be completed within two to three months


p { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } “I am interested in finding a good partner for Pioneer. I am not looking for any particular solution, whether it be Italian or foreign,” says Federico Ghizzoni, CEO of UniCredit, in an interview with Il Sole – 24 Ore, at the Davos conference. He says that the sales process is underway: “we are expecting bids in the next few weeks, and are planning to complete the sale one way or another in the next two to three months.”
