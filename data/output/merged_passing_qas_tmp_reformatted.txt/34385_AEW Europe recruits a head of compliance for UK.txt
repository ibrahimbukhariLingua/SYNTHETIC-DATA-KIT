AEW Europe recruits a head of compliance for UK


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The asset management firm AEW Europe has announced the recruitment of Philip Olmer as executive director, head of Legal & Compliance for its London office. Before joining AEW Europe, Olmer was a partner at Nabarro LLP, a leading real estate law firm. AEW Europe has over EUR18bn in assets under management.
