WisdomTree appoints new head of bond research for Europe


WisdomTree has recruited Lidia Treiber as head of bond research for Europe. Treiber had for the past four years worked as a bond specialist for Invesco ETFs. Before that, she worked at Wellington Management and BlackRock. Treiber will report to Christopher Gannatti, head of research for Europe, and will collaborate directly with the analysis teams based in London and the United States.
