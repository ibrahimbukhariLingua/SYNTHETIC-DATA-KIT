Santander AM adds to equity management team


Santander AssetManagement (Santander AM) is continuing to add to its management team, with the recruitment of Gonzalo Sanz as senior analyst responsible for small and midcaps, the website Funds Society reports. Sanz will begin in July, and will work on the team of Lola Solana, equity manager responsible for small and midcaps. Sanz, who has 14 years of experience, joins from Mirabaud Securities, where he had been working for 11 years as an equity analyst.
