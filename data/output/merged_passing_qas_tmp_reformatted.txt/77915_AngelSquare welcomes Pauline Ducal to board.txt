AngelSquare welcomes Pauline Ducal to board


The startup investment community AngelSquare has announced that Pauline Duval, CEO of the Duval Group, will be joining its board. “The spirit of our family group has led us to pay great attention to the world around us, and to the sustainable development challenges related to it. This engagement is for the long term. Joining the board at AngelSquare is an opportunity to continue to support startups as an entrepreneur, and also as an investor, for positive environmental, social and economic impact,” says Duval in a statement. The Duval group, founded by Eric Duval and co-directed with his daughter, Pauline Duval, is a French family business in the real estate industry. It has over 3,000 employees, and earnings of over €700m in 2018.
