Robeco loses head of United Kingdom activities


Mark Barry, previously head of Robeco in the United Kingdom and head of the institutional activity at Robeco UK, left the Netherlands asset management firm at the end of 2016, according to his LinkedIn profile. He joined Robeco in September 2015 from Russell Investments, where he had been managing director of the department dedicated to retirement solutions for institutionals. His LinkedIn profile states that Barry has launched his own advising firm, entitled Oysterbed Advisors Limited, registered with the British Companies House on 23 December 2016.At Robeco UK, Peter Walsh, head of consulltant relationships, has been appointed as interim head of institutional activities in the United Kingdom, replacing Barry, according to the website of the asset management firm.
