Fondsdepot Bank widens its remit


p { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } DWPT Deutsche Weltpapiertreuhand GmbH is the «new distribution partner” of Fondsdepot Bank. The bank, an affiliate of Xchanging, becomes the exclusive provider for management on its open platform of investment accounts for clients of DWPT and its 45 partners.With the new agreement, Fondsdepot Bank consolidates its position as the largest independent provider of investment account administration in Germany, with more than 1.7 million accounts and about EUR31bn in assets under administration.
