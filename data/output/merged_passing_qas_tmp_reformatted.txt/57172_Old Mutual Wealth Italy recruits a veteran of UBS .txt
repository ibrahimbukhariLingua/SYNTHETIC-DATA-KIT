Old Mutual Wealth Italy recruits a veteran of UBS AM


The Flavia group, controlled by Cinven, has recruited Luca Matassino as head of sales, responsible for networks at its affiliate Old Mutual Wealth italy, Bluerating reports. Before that, Matassino had been executive director and deputy head of wholesale in Italy at UBS Asset Management, responsible for distribution channels, and head of advising. Before that, he spent seven years at Fidelity International.
