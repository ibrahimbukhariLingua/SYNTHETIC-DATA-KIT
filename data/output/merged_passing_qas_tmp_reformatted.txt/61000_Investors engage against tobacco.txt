Investors engage against tobacco


At a global tobacco-free day, a coalition of institutional investors, representing USD3.8trn in assets, has published a joint declaration in which it encourages governments to continue their efforts to combat smoking, The leading signatories include Axa, Scor, CalPERS and AMP Capital. Other signatories include La Financière de l’Echiquier, Mirova, BNP Paribas Cardif, Caisse des Dépôts, Candriam, and the Fonds de réserve pour les retraites.Every year, tobacco kills more than 7 million people worldwide, largely in developing countrires, and costs the economy USD1trn.
