Gramercy Funds Management hires an economist


Gramercy Funds Management, a USD5billion dedicated emerging markets investment manager, has hired Sarah Glendon to the new position of senior vice president, sovereign economist. Her emerging markets sovereign risk expertise will enhance Gramercy’s ability to identify and interpret global market trends and movements, as well as their underlying influencing factors, according to a press statement. Her addition brings the credit research team to 15 analysts. Prior to joining Gramercy, Sarah Glendon was a senior analyst and vice president in Moody’s Sovereign Risk Group where she served as a lead analyst on the Latin American Sovereign Team.
