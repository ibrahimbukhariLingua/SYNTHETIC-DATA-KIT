New vice chairman at Skagen


P { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } Tim Warrington has been appointed as vice chairman and CEO of Skagen Fonder, after one of the four founders of the firm, Åge Westbø, left his job on 31 May. Westbø had been responsible for relationships with clients and led Skagen’s activities in Norway, Sweden, Denmark and internationally. But he remains a shareholder and partner. Warrington had previously been responsible both for the international activity of Skagen and advising to clients in Norway. He began in his new role on 1 June. Skagen has begun the search for a replacement for these two positions.
