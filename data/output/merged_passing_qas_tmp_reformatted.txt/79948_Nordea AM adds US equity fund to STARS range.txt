Nordea AM adds US equity fund to STARS range


Nordea Asset Management has launchedthe Nordea 1 – North American Stars Equity fund, upping to six the number of funds available within its STARS fund range that focuses on ESG criteria. The new strategy leverages thetrack record of the Nordea 1 – Global Stars Equity fund and relies on the firm’s STARS approach that combines high ESG standards with proactive engagement to generate alpha within the US equities space. Thishigh conviction stock-pickingfund is run by Johan Swahn, a member of the Fundamental Equities Team, who also is portfolio manager of the Nordea 1 – Global Stars Equity fund. It tallies some $350min assets under management.
