Danish pension funds increasingly focus on climate


@page { margin: 0.79in }p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 115% }Forthe fourth time since 2013, WWF Denmark has analysed the way in whichDanish pension funds integrate climate issues into their investmentdecisions, the Danish website AMWatch reports. The results areencouraging, as funds are increasingly taking these issues intoaccount. Eight out of 17 pension funds studied refer to the Parisclimate agreement. A growing number of pension funds are alsobeginning to make their exclusion lists public. However, they are notso ready to reveal the companies with which they have engagementpolicies.
