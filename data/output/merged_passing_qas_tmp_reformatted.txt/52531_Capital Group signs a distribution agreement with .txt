Capital Group signs a distribution agreement with Banca Sella


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Capital Group has signed an agreement with the Italian Banca Sella group to distribute the full product range from the US asset management firm licensed for sale in Italy, via 400 financial advisers at Banca Patrimoni Sella & C. The range is composed of nine long-only equity funds, six bond funds, and three multi-asset class funds, all of them actively-managed, with a proprietary management system entitled Capital System.
