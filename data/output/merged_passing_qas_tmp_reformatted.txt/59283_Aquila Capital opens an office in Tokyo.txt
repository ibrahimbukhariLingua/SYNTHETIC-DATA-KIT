Aquila Capital opens an office in Tokyo


The German alternative asset management firm Aquila Capital has announced the opening of a new office in Tokyo, Japan, to accelerate its investments in the area of renewable energies, and particularly solar energy. Yasuhiro Odajima is appointed as investment manager energy & infrastructure for Asia-Pacific, the asset management firm has announced. Odajima will be based in Tokyo, and joins from Pictet Asset Management. Aquila Capital has been active in the Japanese market for several years, with its first investment fund domiciled in Japan created in 2013. The firm began investing in Japanese solar projects in 2014.
