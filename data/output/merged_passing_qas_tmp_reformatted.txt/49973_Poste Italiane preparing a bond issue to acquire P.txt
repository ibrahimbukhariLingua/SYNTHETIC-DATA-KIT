Poste Italiane preparing a bond issue to acquire Pioneer


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Poste Italiane is studying a bond issue of EUR2bn to finance a potential acquisition of Pioneer, the asset management firm of UniCredit, which has been put up for sale, Bluerating reports, citing Il Sole 24 Ore. The share offering would take place within 6 months following the acquisition, if Poste (which with Anima and CDP is presenting a bid) is able to beat competition from Amundi, Macquarie and Aberdeen. Meanwhile, as the deadline for bids for Pioneer has been postponed until 10 November, Poste is also working on a bridging loan. This would come from JP Morgan, Banca IMI and two or three other banking groups.
