BNP Paribas IP adds to multi-asset class team


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } BNP Paribas Investment Partners (BNPP IP), the asset management arm of the BNP Paribas group, on 7 October announced the reecruitment of Colin Harte as portfolio manager and strategist in the Multi-Asset Solutions department, led by Charles Janssen. Harte will be based in London and will join the Tactical Asset Allocation & Research team, led by chief investment officer Colin Graham. Harte, who has over 30 years of experience in currency and fixed income markets, previously spent 11 years at Barings Asset Management as director of fixed interest and currency and head of absolute return/high alpha group. He has also served in senior position at NatWest/Gartmore Investment Management and Norwich Union Investment Management.
