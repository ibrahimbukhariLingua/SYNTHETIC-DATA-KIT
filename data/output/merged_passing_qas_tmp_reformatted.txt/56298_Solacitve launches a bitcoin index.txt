Solacitve launches a bitcoin index


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Three weeks after bitcoin futures were approved by the Commodity Futures Trading Commission in the United States, Solactive is launching the Solactive Bitcoin Front Month Rolling Futures 5D Index, an index which tracks the performance of XBT futures listed on CBOE Global Markets (CBOE). The index may be used as an underlying asset for ETFs or structured products.
