Natixis GAM changes name of its index-based strategy specialist division


Natixis Global Asset Management has renamed its division specialised in index-based funds, Active Investment Advisors, as Active Index Advisors, a statement released in the United States has announced. The name change comes with no changes in terms of shareholders, staff or processes. The change aims to “better describe its engagement to combine the benefits of active and index-based management for investors,” Natixis GAM explains. The division now known as Active Index Advisors, founded in 2002, includes index-based strategies, including managed accounts and funds, core beta and smart bets, equity portfolios, and ETFs.
