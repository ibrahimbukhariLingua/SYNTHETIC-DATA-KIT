Wisdom Tree launches a fund dedicated to European small caps


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Wisdom Tree has launched the Wisdom Tree Europe Hedged Smallcap Equity Fund on NYSE Arca, a strategy which offers exposure to European small caps with euro hedging. The fund charges 0.58%. The WisdowmTree Europe Hedged Equity Fund (HEDJ), which offers hedged exposure to European large caps, has assets under manaegment of about USD12bn.
