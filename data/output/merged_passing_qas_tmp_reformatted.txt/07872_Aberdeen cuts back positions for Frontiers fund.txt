Aberdeen cuts back positions for Frontiers fund


Investment Week reports that Aberdeen has halved the number of positions for its European Frontiers fund (GBP52m), from 60 to 28. Country and sectoral allocations for the fund have also been modified. One year ago, the fund was 53% exposed to Russia, and 14% to Turkey.
