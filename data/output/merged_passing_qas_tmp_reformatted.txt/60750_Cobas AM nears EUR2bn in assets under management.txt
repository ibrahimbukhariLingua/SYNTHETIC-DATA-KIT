Cobas AM nears EUR2bn in assets under management


Cobas AM, which has just topped EUR1bn in assets under management (NewsManagers of 12 June 2017) for strategies offered in open-ended funds, has won institutional mandates totalling EUR800m. In other words, the firm, founded by Francisco Garcia Paramés is nearing EUR2bn only four months after its launch. The growth is related to the track record of Paramés, a well-known player in the value management universe.Cobas AM is preparing to launch a new fund which will be 75% dedicated to Iberian equities, and which will be eligible for PEA investment. According to Veronica Vieira, country head for France and Benelux, the launch may come in the next few months.
