BankInvest launches a Danish small caps fund


BankInvest has launched a new fund of Danish small caps, Small Cap Danske Aktier. The fund has raised DKK100m (EUR13.4m), which will be invested in Danish start-ups which are not art of the OMC25 index. 50% of capital comes from the Danish Growth Fund, a Danish government fund which aims to finance the economy, and the remaining 50% comes from private investors (221 clients of financial institutions). Bankinvest hopes to raise more capital from private investors. As soon as the fund has DKK300m in assets from investors, the Danish fund will invest DKK200m.
