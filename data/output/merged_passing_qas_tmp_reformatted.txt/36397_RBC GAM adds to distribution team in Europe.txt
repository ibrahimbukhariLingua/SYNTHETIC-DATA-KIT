RBC GAM adds to distribution team in Europe


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } RBC Global Asset Management has appointed Anne-Sophie Girault as managing director for development of activities in Europe, the Middle East and Africa (EMEA). Girault, who will be based in London, joins from Aviva Investors, where she had overseen teams responsible for fixed income clients. She will be responsible more particularly for developing activities serving institutional clients. She will report to Paul Williams, head of RBC GAM for EMEA. Two other recruitments will reinforce institutional distribution and customer services: Oliver Cooper, associate director, responsible for development of EMEA activities, and Fajr Al Tamimi, marketing associate, who will also be responsible for development in the EMEA region.
