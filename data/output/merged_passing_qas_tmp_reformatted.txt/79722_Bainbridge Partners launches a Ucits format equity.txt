Bainbridge Partners launches a Ucits format equity strategy


The quantitative asset management firm Bainbridge Partners, based in London, on 20 January announced the launch of an international Ucits format equity market neutral multi-strategy fund. The Lux Multimanager Sicav- Bainbridge Equity Market Neutral offers exposure to short- and mid-term systematic equity strategies, via a highly diversified international long/short portfolio selected from a universe of 4,000 stocks. The strategy aims for net absolute returns of 6% to 10%, for total volatility of 6% to 8%. The fund, currently available in euros with dollar hedging, is a sub-fund of the LuxMultimanager Sicav, registered in Luxembourg and Ucits-compliant.
