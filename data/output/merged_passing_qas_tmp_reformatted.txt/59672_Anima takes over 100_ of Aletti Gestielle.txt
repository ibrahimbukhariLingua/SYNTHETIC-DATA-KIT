Anima takes over 100% of Aletti Gestielle


Italy’s asset management company Anima Holding is to buy 100% of Aletti Gestielle, the asset management company of the Italian bank Banco BPM for an amount in cash of 700m EUR. The bank could receive up to EUR250m in additional payments.The deal includes a strategic partnership between Banco BPM and Anima and sales deals for the distribution of products for 20 years.With Aletti Gestielle, Anima, which managers more than EUR75bn, adds EUR18bn and becomes the fourth largest player in asset management in Italy. To finance the deal, Anima could raise up to EUR300m. Banco BPM, which holds 15 % of Anima, will stay a shareholder in the new group.
