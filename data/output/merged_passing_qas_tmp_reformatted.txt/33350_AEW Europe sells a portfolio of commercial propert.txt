AEW Europe sells a portfolio of commercial properties to Primonial REIM


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } AEW Europe, a real estate asset management affiliate of Natixis Global Asset Management, has sold its portolio of six stores occupied by the Picard brand throughout France to Primonial REIM, on behalf of Patrimmo Commerce. The operation was carried out on behalf of the Foncière vehicle, which is owned by the OPCI AEW Foncière Ecureuil and managed by AEW Europe SGP. The sale price has not been disclosed.
