Changing of the guard at Credit Suisse


Bruno Aguilar has been relieved of his duties as CEO for management at Credit Suisse Asset Management for Spain, as part of a reshuffle following the acquisition of Aberdeen Asset Management for GBP250m, Funds People reports. The new head of management for Spain, Portugal and Latin America is Pedro Domenech, who created the asset management activities of Lehman Brothers in Spain.
