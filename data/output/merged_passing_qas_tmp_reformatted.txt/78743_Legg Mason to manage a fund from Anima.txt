Legg Mason to manage a fund from Anima


The US asset management firm Legg Mason has signed a partnership with Italy’s Anima to manage a new bond fund of funds, entitled Anima Legg Mason Multi Credit. Western Asset Management, the affiliate of Legg Mason dedicated to bonds, will manage the sub-fund. Anima Legg Mason Multi Credit will have a “flexible and dynamic” approach, and will be diversified both from a geographical and sectoral standpoint, a statement released in Italy says. It will invest in various bond strategies from Legg Mason. Currently, the fund is exposed to investment grade and high yield companies, structured credit, and emerging markets in local currencies. The product will be sold by Anima’s distributors via the fund of fund platform Sistema Open.
