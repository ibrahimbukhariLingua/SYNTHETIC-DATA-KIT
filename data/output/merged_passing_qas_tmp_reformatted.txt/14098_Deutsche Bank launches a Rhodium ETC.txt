Among the 12 products admitted to trading on the Xetra electronic trading platform from Deutsche BÃ¶rse on May 27 is the DB Physical Rhodium ETC Euro (DE000A1KJHG8), which charges fees of 0.95% and replicates the evolution of Rhodium prices in euros.
