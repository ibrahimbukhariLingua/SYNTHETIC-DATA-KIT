Assets in Luxembourg OPC vehicles up by nearly 10% in 12 months


As of 31 July 2017, the overall net assets at collective investment organisms, including OPC vehicles subject to the 2010 law, specialist investment funds and SICARs, totalled EUR3.957581trn, compared with EUR3.943598trn as of 30 June 2017, an increase of 0.35% in one month, according to statistics released by the financial sector surveillance commission (CSSF). Considered over the past twelve months, net asset volume is up 9.84%. The Luxembourg OPC industry has posted a positive variation in July of EUR29.150bn (0.74%), while the unfavourable evolution of the financial markets has totalled EUR15.167bn (-0.39%).
