Kempen Capital Management adds to socially responsible investment team


The Netherlands-based asset management firm Kempen Capital Management on14 May announced the appointment of Eszter Vitorino Fuleky as senior socially responsible investment adviser. As part of the team, Vitorino Fuleky will focus primarily on governance issues. Vitorino Fuleky had previously been head of relationships with financial market players for the Global Reporting Initiative (GRI). GRI is a non-government organisation which develops reporting standards for sustainable development, in partnership with companies, NGOs and academics.
