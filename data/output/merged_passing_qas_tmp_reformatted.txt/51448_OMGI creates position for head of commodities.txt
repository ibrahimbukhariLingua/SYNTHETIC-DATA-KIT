OMGI creates position for head of commodities


Old Mutual Global Investors (OMGI), which is owned by Old Mutual Wealth, has appointed Diego Parrilla to the newly-created position of managing director, responsible for commodities. Parrilla will be based in Singapore, and will report to Paul Simpson, chief investment officer at OMGI. Parrilla will initially work to promode and develop the Old Mutual Gold & Silver funds, whose assets under management total GBP60m. He will also work with OMGI to identify absolute return strategies for precious metals and commodities appropriate to the strategic orientations of the firm.Parrilla previously worked at Dymon Asia Capital, after serving in various roles at BlueCrest Capital Management, Merrill Lynch, Goldman Sachs and JP Morgan.
