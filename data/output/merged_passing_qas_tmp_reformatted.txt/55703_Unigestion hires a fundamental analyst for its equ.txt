Unigestion hires a fundamental analyst for its equity team


The Swiss asset management firm Unigestion has recruited Stefanie Mollin-Elliott as fundamental analyst and senior vice president on its equity team, where she will cover global small and midcap equities.Mollin-Elliott will be based in London, and will report to Bruno Taillardat, executive director and chief investment offiver for the equity team. She will work in collaboration with portfolio managgers to evaluate the equity risk represented in Unigestion equity funds. She also becomes a member of the equity investment board.Mollion-Elliott had previously been a global emerging markets analyst at Allianz Global Investors.
