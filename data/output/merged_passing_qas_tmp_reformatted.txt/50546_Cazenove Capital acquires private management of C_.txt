Cazenove Capital acquires private management of C. Hoare &amp; Co


Cazenove Capital Management, the private management affiliate of Schroders in the United Kingdom, on 3 October announced that it has signed an agreement to acquire the private wealth management division of C. Hoare & Co, a private bank based in London. The private management activity now has 1,800 clients and GBP2.2bn in assets under management. “This agreement will allow C. Hoare & Co to concentrate on its historic private banking activities, while the Schroders group will continue to develop in private management in the United Kingdom,” the two firms say in a joint statement. The transaction, whose price has not been disclosed, is expected to be completed in first quarter 2017.
