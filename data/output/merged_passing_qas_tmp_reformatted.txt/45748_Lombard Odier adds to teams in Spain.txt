Lombard Odier adds to teams in Spain


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The Swiss private bank Lombard Odier is adding to its teams in Spain with the double recruitment of Guillermo Alvarez Marrodan as senior private banker, and Mariano Aldama as financial agent, the specialist website Funds People reports. Alvarez Marrodan, who has over 20 years of experience in the sector, has worked at several top firms, including Merrill Lynch and Credit Suisse. Aldama, with 25 years of experience in the capital markets and debt sector, has worked in Madrid and London at Royal Bank of Scotland, UBS, Deutsche Bank and JP Morgan.
