Achmea IM recruits a manager from Swiss Life


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Herman Barten this month joined the asset management firm Achmea Investment Management (Achmea IM) as account manager responsible for institutional clients, the specialist website Fondsnieuws reports. Barten worked for one year at Zwitserleven (Swiss Life Netherlands) after serving at Kepler Chevreux. From 2008 to 2011, he worked at Axa Investment Management. From 1992 to 2006, he worked for the banking and then asset management units of the ABN Amro group.
