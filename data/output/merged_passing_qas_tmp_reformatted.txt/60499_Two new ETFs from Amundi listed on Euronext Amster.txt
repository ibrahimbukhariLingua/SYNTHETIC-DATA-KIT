Two new ETFs from Amundi listed on Euronext Amsterdam


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The stock market operator Euronext has announced the listing of two new ETFs from Amundi AM on Euronext Amsterdam from 27 June. The first, Amundi MSCI USA Minimum Volatility Factor Ucits ETF, is based on the MSCI USA Minimum Volatility index, and charges 0.18%. The second, Amundi Global Infrastructure Ucits ETF, is based on the Solactive Global Infra Low Ear index, and charges 0.55%.
