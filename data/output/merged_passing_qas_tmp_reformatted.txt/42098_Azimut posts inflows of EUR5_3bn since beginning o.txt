Azimut posts inflows of EUR5.3bn since beginning of year


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } In the first nine months of the year, the Italian asset management firm Azimut has posted net inflows of EUR5bn. As of the end of October, inflows total EUR5.3bn. Assets totalled EUR34.1bn, of which EUR29.3bn were assets under mangement. As of the end of October, these sums stood at EUR35.3bn and EUR30.2bn. Consolidated net profits at Azimut totalled EUR208.1m, up 68% compared with the corresponding period of 2014. These are the best profit results in the history of Azimut.
