Fund from Rotschild IP acquires Sarawak group


Cabestan Capital 2, the fund managed by the small caps team at Edmond de Rothschild Investment Partners (Edmond de Rothschild IP), is acquiring the Sarawak group from Alliance Entreprendre and ACG Management. The management team, led by Guy Maindiaux, is reinvesting alongside the majority fund, Edmond de Rothschild IP says in a statement. The sale price has not been disclosed. The Sarawak group, founded in 1999 and taken over by Guy Maindiaux in 2006, is a French player specialised in outsourced sales and management of promotional operations, aimed at gross consumer clients. The group has over 300 employees, and made earnings of EUR18m in 2016-2017.
