GAM Investments adds to team dedicated to emerging market credit


GAM Investments adds to its team responsible for credit for emerging markets with the recruitment of Markus Heider from 2 July. The specialist will be based in London, and has worked in the City as head of inflation research for Deutsche Bank, where he began his career in 2000, a statement says. At the end of March 2018, the team responsible for emerging market credit had about USD11bn in assets under management.
