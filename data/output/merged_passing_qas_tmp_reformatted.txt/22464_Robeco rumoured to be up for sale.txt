Robeco rumoured to be up for sale


Rabobank is reviewing strategic options for its fund management affiliate Robeco, including a potential sale, Reuters reports. A spokesperson for the Netherlands-based establishment has announced that the firm is studing “strategic options” for its affiliate Robeco, confirming reports in the local press. Het Financieele Dagblad reports, citing anonymous sources, that Rabobank is hoping to sell Robeco for a total price of EUR1.5bn to EUR2bn, and that it has retained Deutsche Bank and JP Morgan as advisory banks.
