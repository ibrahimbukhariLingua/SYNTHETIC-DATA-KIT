Flossbach von Storch opens a branch office in Italy


The German asset management firm Flossbach von Storch (EUR36bn in assets) has opened a branch office in Milan, Bluerating reports. The launch comes via the Luxembourg affiliate Flossbach von Storch Invest S.A. The Milanese arm will focus on the development of local distribution channels for the wholesale segment, mainly via agreements with banking establishments. The Italian office will be led by Arcangelo Barletta, country head, who joined Flossbach in May this year.
