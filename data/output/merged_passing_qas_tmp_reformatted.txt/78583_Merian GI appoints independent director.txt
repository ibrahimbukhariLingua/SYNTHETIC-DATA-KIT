Merian GI appoints independent director


Merian Global Investors has appointed Catherine Cripps as independent non-executive director. She will also replace Mark Gregory as chairman of the risk and auditing committee, following his appointment as CEO of the firm. She will also be responsible for helping to build the growth strategy for the firm, and will work with the chairman, the board of directors and the management of Merian GI. As chair of the auditing and risk committee, she will apply her independent vision to the oversight and governance of the structure. Cripps has over 30 years of experience in the finance industry. She is currently curator of the Nuclear Liabilities Fund, and non-executive director of the asset management firm CQS Management. In the past, she has worked at several companies, including GAM, Aida Capital, and Credit Suisse.
