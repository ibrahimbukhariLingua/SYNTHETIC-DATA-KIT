T. Rowe Price adds to sales team in Switzerland


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Finews reports that the US asset management firm T. Rowe Price will be adding to its teams in Switzerland. The first recruitment is about to be finalized; it is for a position on the wholesale specialist sales team. The next recruitment to add to this team is in progress, but will come only when the first person has fully joined the team. Paolo Corredig, head of intermediary business for Switzerland, also stated that the firm is searching for “key positions” for the Swiss, German, Italian and Spanish markets.
