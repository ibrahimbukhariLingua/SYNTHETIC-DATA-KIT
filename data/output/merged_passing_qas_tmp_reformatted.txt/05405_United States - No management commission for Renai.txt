United States - No management commission for Renaissance Institutional Futures


James Simons, head of Renaissance Technologies (USD25bn) has announced that subscribers to Renaissance Institutional Futures (USD3bn in assets) will not be charged a 1% management commission in 2009, following losses of 12% for the fund in 2008, even if the hedge fund performs well this year, the Wall Street Journal reports. In addition, the fund will have to compensate for its losses before it will be allowed to charge performance commissions.
