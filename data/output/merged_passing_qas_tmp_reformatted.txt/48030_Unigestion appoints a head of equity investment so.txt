Unigestion appoints a head of equity investment solutions


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Unigestion has recruited Rudyard Ekindi in Geneva as head of investment solutions for its equity team. Ekindi, who joined in December 2014, reports to Alexei Jourovski, managing director and head of equities, and will aim to meet the investment needs of Unigestion clients and to construct original and custom asset management solutions to meet their expectations. Ekindi previously served as director if investment research at the National Employment Savings Trust (NEST), where he was responsible for steering the investment team and designing investment strategy for the pension fund. He was previously head of asset allocation at Credit Suisse Asset Management, responsible in particular for cross-asset solutions for institutional clients, and then on the quantitative strategies team as director of research.
