Northern Trust looks to grow in Asia


P { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } The US group Northern Trust would like to continue its growth in Asia, where it already has a strong presence in India, Asian Investor reports. Northern Trust is planning to establish a presence in the Philippines next year, probably in second half. It is also studying potential opportunities to develop in the family office segment. It is planning to offer custody services to Chinese clients, and to strengthen its client base in South Korea.
