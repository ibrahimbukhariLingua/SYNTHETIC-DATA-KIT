Allianz GI launches an Asian equity UCITS hedge fund


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Allianz Global Investors has recently launched a UCITS format hedge fund strategy dedicated to equities from the Asia-Pacific region, acording to Absolut Report. The Allianz Discovery Asia Strategy (ISIN LU0901926393) offers the same strategy as the Allianz GI fund of the same name, via a swap. The fund charges 1.20% per year. The minimal investment is USD1m.
