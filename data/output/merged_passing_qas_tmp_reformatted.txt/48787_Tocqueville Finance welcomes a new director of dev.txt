Tocqueville Finance welcomes a new director of development


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Tocqueville Finance has announced the arrival of Pascale Attuil as director of development for distribution and institutionals. In this role, she joins the management board at Tocqueville Finance. Attuil reports to Hervé Guiriec, CEO of Tocqueville Finance. Attuil, 59, began his career in 1985 at the Viel group as an inter-bank market agent, and then in 1989 became director of sales at Tradition Lausanne Paris. From 1992 to 2000, she joined Financor as senior sales broker in charge of derivative products, swaps and options for large French and international institutionals. From 2001 to 2007, Attuil joined Barep Asset Management, an alternative management affiliate of Société Générale, where she became director of sales for France in 2002. In 2007, she joined Société Générale Asset Management, where she was appointed as director of corporate sales for France and Europe, and then director of sales and distribution for Europe from 2008 to 2012 (Amundi since January 2010). In 2013, she joined the board at Riskelia, a research and management company specialised in deciphering speculative behaviour and detecting extreme shocks. She was responsible for development serving institutional investors in France and Europe.
