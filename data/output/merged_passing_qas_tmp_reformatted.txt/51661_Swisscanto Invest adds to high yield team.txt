Swisscanto Invest adds to high yield team


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Swisscanto Invest has announced the recruitment of Susanne Kundert as an addition to its team dedicated to high yield. She becomes portfolio manager, and will be responsible for managing several high yield portfolios. She joins Swisscanto Invest after seven years at LGT Capital Partners in the position of head of the Credit & Sustainability division. Before that, she worked at Man Group.
