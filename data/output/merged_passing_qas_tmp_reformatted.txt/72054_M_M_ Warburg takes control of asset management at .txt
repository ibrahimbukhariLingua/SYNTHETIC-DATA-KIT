M.M. Warburg takes control of asset management at Nord/LB


The private bank M.M. Warburg, based in Hamburg, has announced that it is taking over the asset management activities of the public regional bank Nord/LB, with a stake of 75.1% in its capital, and the regional bank retaining 24.9%. The terms of the transaction, which is still subject to approval bt the supervisory authorities, have not been disclosed.The new asset management firm will have assets under management and administration of over EUR34bn, mostly from institutional clients.
