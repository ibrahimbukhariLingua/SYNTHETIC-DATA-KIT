International accounting assumptions: Impact of new actuarial tables in Switzerland


In this issue of Hot Topics, we provide an overview of the potential impacts of the new BVG/LPP 2010 tables in Switzerland on pension liabilities and costs under international accounting standards. We focus in particular on the possibility now of using generational mortality tables which allow for expected future improvements in longevity; the use of such tables could lead to significant increases in liabilities and costs compared to the tables typically used currently.
