Pimco recruits a credit specialist


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The US asset management firm Pimco on 5 August announced the recruitment of Ethan Schwartz as executive vice president and portfolio manager dedciated to distressed credit. Schwartz, who joined the New York office of Pimco this week, will report to Sai Devabhaktuni, executive vice president and head of corporate distressed portfolio management. Schwartz has more than 15 years of experience as an analyst and manager in the area of stressed credit and distressed investments. Before joining Pimco, he served as a senior analyst at Contrarian Capital Management, where he helped to supervise USD2.7bn in hedge fund assets. Before that, he worked at CRT Capital Group, Tribeca Investments and Goldman Sachs, respectively.
