Bond funds caught up in a storm


Contradictory news circulating about Greece and its future in the euro zone, statements by the managing director of the International Monetary Fund, Christine Lagarde, and by the head of the ECB, Mario Draghi, and the resulting erratic movements of the Bund have not helped to reinsure bond investors. In the first few days of June, European bond funds posted highly significant redemptions for the fourth time in the past five weeks, EPFR Global reports.However, funds dedicated to European equities attracted a further USD1bn in the week ending 3 June, despite concerns over Greece. And Chinese equity funds have continued in the positive course set in previous weeks. In total, equity funds have posted net inflows of about USD5bn in the week under review. Bod funds attracted USD2bn, while money market funds attracted slightly over USD1bn.
