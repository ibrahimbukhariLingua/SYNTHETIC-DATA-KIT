Invesco reduces management fees for two PowerShares ETFs


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The US asset management firm Invesco on 22 December announced a decision to reduce management fees for its ETF PowerShares S&P 500 Momentum Portfolio and PowerShares S&P 500 Enhanced Value Portfolio. In both cases, fees will be reduced from 0.25% to 0.13%, from 1 January 2018. “We constantly evaluate our product offerings in order to ensure that we offer the most inexpensive investment solutions to meet the needs of our investors,” says Dan Draper, global head of ETFs at Invesco, in a statement.
