Euro zone bond funds attracted investors in June


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The passing months are adding up for collective asset management in France. According to data from Europerformance-SIX Telekurs for June, French-registered funds have posted net outflows of EUR10.86bn (a variation in assets of -1.41%), for which the money market asset class is nearly single-handedly responsible. Net outflows from these categories totalled EUR10.70bn (a decline in assets under management of 3.36%). In a virtually symbolic manner compared to outflows, all equity funds also posted outflows. They lost EUR42.72m (a decline in assets under management of 1.11%). Only bond funds convinced investors. Overall, these posted net subscriptions of EUR1.39bn.
