UBP recruits a former banker from UBS


@page { size: 8.27in 11.69in; margin: 0.79in }p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 115%; background: transparent }Union Bancaire Privée (UBP) has recruited Urs Zeltner, a former banker from UBS. Zeltner has already begun in Zurich, as senior managing director, according to an internal memo obtained by finews. Zeltner will report to Adrian Künzi, head of UBP Zurich since the beginning of the year, and will assist in the development of the activities of the bank in Zurich.In recent years, Zeltner had been vice chairman for wealth management Europe at UBS.
