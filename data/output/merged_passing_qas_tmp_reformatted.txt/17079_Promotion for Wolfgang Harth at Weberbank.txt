Promotion for Wolfgang Harth at Weberbank


p { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } The Berlin private bank Weberbank Actiengesellschaft, an affiliate of the Mittelbrandenburgische Sparkasse, has announced that Wolfgang Harth, who previously had spent four years in management positions, has been appointed as a member of the managing board in charge of high net worth private clients and centres of expertise for real estate and insurance. Harth joins Klaus Siegers, chairman of the board, in charge of strategy, personnel, asset management and institutional clients, and Stephan Brandt (finance, controlling, risk control).
