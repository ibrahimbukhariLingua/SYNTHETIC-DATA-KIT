Vontobel AM recruits a sales manager in Milan


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Vontobel Asset Management has recruited Giovanna Verosimile in Milan as sales & relationship manager for its retail activity in Italy. Verosimile joins from Banca Albertini Syz, where she had been sales support. Before that, she had been at Oyster Funds, also in Milan.
