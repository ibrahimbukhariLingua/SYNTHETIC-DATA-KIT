Santander AM signs agreement with BlackRock to use Aladdin


The Spanish asset management firm Santander Asset Management has signed an agreement with BlackRock to use the Aladdin platform, Funds People reports. The Aladdin IT and technology platform from BlackRock is dedicated to asset management firms, and offers them a variety of services, including data, risk and compliance management. It also allows for companies to obtain assistance with their IT and digital transitions. Santander AM will become the first asset management firm to use Aladdin in Spain, Brazil, Portugal and Argentina. The platform will be deployed first in Spain, the United Kingdom, Portugal, Luxembourg, and Poland from April 2020; then in Brazil and Argentina from August 2020, and finally in Mexico, Chile and Puerto Rico from December 2020. Credit Suisse has also recently tapped Aladdin to implement a unified digital structure.
