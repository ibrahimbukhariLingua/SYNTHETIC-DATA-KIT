DWS Investment merges funds


P { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } DWS Investment is merging the DWS FlexProfit 80, FlexProfit 85 and FlexProfit 90 funds, which will be integrated into the new DWS Funds Global Protect 90 fund. The product is available in Germany, Austria and Italy. The DWS Vermögensmandat-Protekt 80 fund will be absorbed into the DWS Vermögensmandat-Balance fund, Fondsweb adds.
