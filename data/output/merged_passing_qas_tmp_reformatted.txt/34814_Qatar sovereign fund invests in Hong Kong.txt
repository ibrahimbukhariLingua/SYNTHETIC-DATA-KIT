Qatar sovereign fund invests in Hong Kong


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The Qatar Investment Authority (QIA), the sovereign fund of Qatar, will acquire a stake in the major Hong Kong retail operator Lifestyle International Holdings. According to a statement from Lifestyle, the sovereign fund will spend HKD4.78bn, or about USD616m, for a 20% stake in the publicly-traded firm. QIA acquired the stake from Real Reward, which will remain the largest shareholder in Lifestyle, with 32.2% of capital. Real Reward is 50% controlled by Chow Tai Fook Enterprises, controlled by the Hong Kong billionaire Cheng Yu-tung.
