UBS Wealth Management Americas teams up with fintechs


UBS Wealth Management Americas has signed a partnership with the US fintech firm SigFig, a specialist in robo-advising and wealth management IT solutions. They are developing a financial technology together for UBS Wealth Management Americas (WMA) and its financial advisers and clients, and will set up a joint research and innovation centre to collaborate on new asset management tools, to be knows as the «Advisor Technology Research and Innovation Lab», a statement says. As part of the alliance, UBS WMA will acquire a stake in SigFig, of an undisclosed amount.
