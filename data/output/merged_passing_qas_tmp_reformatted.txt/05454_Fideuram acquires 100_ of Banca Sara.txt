Fideuram acquires 100% of Banca Sara


p { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } Banca Fideuram has acquired 100% of Banca Sara, for a symbolic price of 1 euro, Il Sole – 24 Ore reports. With 460 financial advisers for all of Italy and assets of EUR2.7bn, Banca Sara is the twelfth-largest financial advisers’ network in Italy. In 2009, the bank posted net subscriptions of EUR250m, while net assets totalled over EUR340m in the first nine months of 2010.
