Irish stock exchange launches a fund information website


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; }a:link { } The Irish Stock Exchange (ISE) has launched ISE Fund Hub, an electronic resource providing information about all funds listed on the Irish stock market. The new website, at www.isefundhub.com, includes all relevant information about funds, key documents, performance analysis, and profiles of managers. ISE Fund Hub, dedicated to professionals, was developed in partnership with the Danish fund infrastructure provider FundConnect, which has developed a similar platform for the Scandinavian countries.
