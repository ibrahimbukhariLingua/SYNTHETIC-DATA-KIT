Nordea sells private bank in Luxembourg to UBS


UBS will acquire a part of the private banking activities of Nordea based in Luxembourg, representing EUR13bn in assets as of the end of 2017, the two firms have announced in a statement, confirming rumours in the press (see NewsManagers of 15 January).The operation follows a strategic review of the international private banking activities of Nordea, and a decision to concentrate on Northern European countries.This will allow UBS to accelerate its presence in Europe, and to strengthen its position as a key wealth manager for Northern European clients.Nordea Asset Management will continue to operate in Luxembourg.
