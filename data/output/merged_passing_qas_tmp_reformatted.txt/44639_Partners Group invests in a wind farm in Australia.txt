Partners Group invests in a wind farm in Australia

It will invest AUD450m in the project, in which it will be the largest holding partner, the firm announced in a statement on 26 June. The other partners are Renewable Energy Systems (RES), General Electric, and OPTrust, a Canadian pension fund. The total size of the investment has not been disclosed. The Ararat wind farm will be constructed in the region of Melbourne, in the state of Victoria. It was initiated by RES. Construction is to begin very soon. The wind farm will be completed in mid-2017, and will include 75 turbines, with a total capacity of 240 MW, which corresponds to the average consumption of 123,000 households.
