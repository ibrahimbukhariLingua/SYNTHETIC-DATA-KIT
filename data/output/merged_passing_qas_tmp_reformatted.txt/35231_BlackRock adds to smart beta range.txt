BlackRock adds to smart beta range


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } iShares, the ETF unit of BlackRock, will launch a range of ETFs which will allow investors to access indices which are weighted according to criteria other than market capitalisation, Financial News reports. It will be the first time that the US giant offers this type of product in Europe, though a product range of this type already exists in the United States. For this new range, iShares has selected MSCI as an index provider.
