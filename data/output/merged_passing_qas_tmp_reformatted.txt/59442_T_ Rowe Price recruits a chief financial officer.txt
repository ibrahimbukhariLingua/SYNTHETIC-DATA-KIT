T. Rowe Price recruits a chief financial officer


T. Rowe Price Group, which has USD927bn in assets under management, has recruited Céline Dufétel as vice president and future chief financial officer. She will succeed Ken Moreland, who is leaving the firm in first quarter 2018.Dufétel, a Frenchwoman, currently works at Neuberger Berman as managing director and global head of marketing and customer service. She previously spent several years at McKinsey, where she was a partner.At T. Rowe Price, Dufétel will report to CEO Bill Stromberg, and will be based in Baltimore, the headquarters of the firm.
