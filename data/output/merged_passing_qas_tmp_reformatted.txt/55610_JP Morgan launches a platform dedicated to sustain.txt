JP Morgan launches a platform dedicated to sustainable investment


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } JP Morgan has launched JP Morgan Ethos Investments, a platform dedicated to sustainable investment, offering a very wide range of toold aimed at institutional investors and distributors seeking to create custom products which integrate envinromental, social and governance (ESG) factors. Through the numerous partnerships developed by JP Morgan with providers of specialist indices and with asset managers, investors are able to gain exposure to assets selected for their ESG performance. They can select between passive indices, actively-managed funds and custom portfolios, in several asset classes, equities, credit and bonds. JP Morgan will also offer green bonds and social bonds issued by businesses and very highly rated supra-national entities.
