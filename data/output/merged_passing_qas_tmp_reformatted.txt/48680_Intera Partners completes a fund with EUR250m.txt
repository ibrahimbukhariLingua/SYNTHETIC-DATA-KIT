Intera Partners completes a fund with EUR250m


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The venture capital firm Intera Partners, based in Finland, has announced that it has completed its latest fund, Intera Fund III, with EUR250m. Intera Partners says in a statement that it has received engagements from what it calls a “balanced” mix of European investors, including pension funds, funds of funds, and family offices. The previsu fund was closed in 2011 with EUR200m. The new fund will invest primarily in businesses based in Finland with a total corporate value of EUR20m to EUR80m, and with substantial potential for growth in their original markets and/or internationally. The fund may also invest in Swedish companies of a similar size which also offer good growth outlooks.
