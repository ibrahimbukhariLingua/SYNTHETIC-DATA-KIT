Former SSGA manager launches a China hedge fund


Hedge Week reports that the former SSGA manager Wu Huimin has founded a hedge fund specialised in investments in China. He is launching the product in collaboration with the former director of China research at Citigroup, Xue Lan, and the analyst Sun Lu from Indus Capital Global Advisors. The fund is entitled Trivest Advisors Ltd., and has already raised USD93m.
