Chinese sovereign fund CIC appoints a new vice chairman


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; }a:link { } The Chinese sovereign fund China Investment Corporation (CIC) has appointed Liu Jun with effect from 17 November as executive vice-chairman on its board of directors, the specialist website Asia Asset Management reports.
