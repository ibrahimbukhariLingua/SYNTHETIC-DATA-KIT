DeAWM recruits from Barclays Wealth


Deutsche Asset & Wealth Management (DeAWM) has appointed Chris Guttilla as managing director and client adviser. Guttilla, based in New York, will report directly to Brett Kellam, managing director and regional head for private clients. Guttilla, a specialist in high net worth clients and family offices, previously worked at Barclays Wealth, where he ultimately remained 15 years.
