Silverfleet Capital recruits for European teams


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The private equity investment firm SilverFleet Capital on Tuesday, 21 July announced the recruitments of Vincent Clément at Silverfleet Capital in Paris as an associate, and Pater Kisenyi in London as an investment executive, The firm has also announced the appointments of Klaus Maurer and Falko Westphalen, both based in Munich, as principals. Clément, who has an engineering degree from the Ecole Centrale de Lille, previously worked at Lazard in Paris as an associate on the mergers and acquisitions team. Kisenyi, a graduate of the University of Nottingham, previously belonged to the mergers and acquisitions team at Greenhill & Co. Lastly, Maurer and Westphalen joined Silverfleet Capital in 2009 and 2010, respectively, as investment executives.
