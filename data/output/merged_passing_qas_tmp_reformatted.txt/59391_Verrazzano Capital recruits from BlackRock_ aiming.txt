Verrazzano Capital recruits from BlackRock, aiming to double assets in three years


The French asset management firm Verrazzano Capital, a specialist in European alternative and long only equity strategies, with nearly EUR900m in assets as of 31 December 2016, is aiming to double its assets in three years. With this in mind, David Disneur, previously a director at BlackRock, has joined Verrazzano Capital as director of sales. He will work to accelerate development in France and the rest of Europe. “This senior recruitment is a sign of the attractiveness of our firm and of its ambitions for the future. We reaffirm our desire to continue and accelerate our growth, particularly in the French market.”, said Guillaume Rambourg, CEO of Verrazzano Capital.
