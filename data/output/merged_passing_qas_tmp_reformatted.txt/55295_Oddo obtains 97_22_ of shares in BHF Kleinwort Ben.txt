Oddo obtains 97.22% of shares in BHF Kleinwort Benson and launches a squeeze-out

At the closing of the initial acceptance period for the counter-offier of 10 February 2016, 71,234,478 shares in NHF KB had been traded, representing 53.87% of shares in circulation in BHF KB. Equities in BHF KB contributed as part of the counter-offer during the initial acceptance period are due to be paid by 22 February 2016, a statement says. “Under section 7.7.4 of the Prospectus, the Counter-Bidder, who holds more than 95% of equities in BHF KB at closing of the Initial Acceptance Period, has decided to reopen the Counter-Offer on the same conditions and to undertake a squeeze-out offer subject to articles 42 and 43 juncto article 57 of the Royal Decree of 27 April 2007, concerning public buyout offers, and article 513 article 57 of the Royal Decree of 27 April 2007, concerning public buyout offers, and article 513 of the Companies code,” a statement says. The acquisition bid price is EUR5.75 per share in BHF KB, with coupon no. 1 and following attached. The Acceptance Period for the buyout offer runs from 22 February 2016 to 11 March 2016 at 16:00 (CET). An official announcement of the squeeze-out offer will be published in the Belgian press on 17 February 2016.
