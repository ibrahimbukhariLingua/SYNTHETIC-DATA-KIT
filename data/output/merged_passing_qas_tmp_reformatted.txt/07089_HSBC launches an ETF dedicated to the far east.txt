HSBC launches an ETF dedicated to the far east


p { margin-bottom: 0.08in; } The HSBC group is extending its range of ETF products with the launch of an ETF proviging exposure to the far east. The HSBC MSCI EM Far East ETF, which has total TER of 0.6%, replicates the MSCI EM Far East index, which itself includes the market performance of the largest Chinese businesses, based on the markets of Hong Kong, Indonesia, South Korea, Malaysia, the Philippines, Taiwan, and Thailand.
