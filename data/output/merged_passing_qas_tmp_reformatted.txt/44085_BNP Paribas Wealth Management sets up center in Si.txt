BNP Paribas Wealth Management sets up center in Silicon Valley


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Bank of the West Wealth Management, an affiliate of the BNP Paribas banking group, has opened a new center in Palo Alto, California, in the heart of Silicon Valley. This is its twelfth centre dedicated to wealth management in the United States. A team of advisers from the private bank and wealth management professionals will offer all exclusive and personalized wealth management services, to meet the specific needs of high net worth and ultra-high net worth clients of the private bank: retail clients, families, entrepreneurs and heads of companies who live or work in Silicon Valley.
