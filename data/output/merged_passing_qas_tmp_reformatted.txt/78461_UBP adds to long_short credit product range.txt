UBP adds to long/short credit product range


Union Bancaire Privée (UBP) on Monday18 March announced that it is launching a new strategy on its UCITS alternative platform: U Access (IRL) GCA Credit Long/Short UCITS. The long/short credit fund is launched in partnership with Global Credit Advisers (GCA), an alternative asset management firm based in New York, which manages the fund. The fund consists of an actively-managed long/short corporate credit strategy, and is focused primarily on investment grade and high yield bonds and distressed debt. “The strategy uses a fundamental research strategy which relies on a database which has existed for decades, as well as on a team of highly experienced analysts in corporate credit,” UBP says in a statement.
