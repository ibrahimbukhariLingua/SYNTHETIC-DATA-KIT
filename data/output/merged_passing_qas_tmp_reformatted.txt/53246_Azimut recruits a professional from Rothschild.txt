Azimut recruits a professional from Rothschild


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Azimut Wealth Management, the wealth management affiliate of the Italian asset and wealth management firm Azimut, has recruited Paolo Lenzi, a professional specialised in high net worth individuals and ultra high net worth individuals, the specialist website Bluerating reports. Lenzi joins from Rothschild Italy, where he had served as managing director and head of private clients at Rothschild Wealth Management Italy. Azimut Wealth Management has 205 professionals managing assets of over EUR8bn.
