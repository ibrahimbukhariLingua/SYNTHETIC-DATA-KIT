Oddo &amp; Cie recruits a CFO


The Oddo group has announced the appointment of Christophe Tadié as CFO of Oddo & Cie. He will also become a member of the executive board at the Oddo group. He succeeds François Levé, who has been appointed as director of inspection at the group. Since April 2008, Tadié had been a partner at the Bain & Company agency in Paris, where he was head of the Financial Services Practice since 2010. He has advised several European banking groups on their growth strategy or on projects to adapt to the crisis. At the Oddo group, Tadié assisted with the mergers with the Banque d’Orsay and Banque Robeco, a statement says.
