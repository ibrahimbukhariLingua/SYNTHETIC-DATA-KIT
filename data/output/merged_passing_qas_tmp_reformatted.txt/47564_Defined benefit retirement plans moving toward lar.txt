Defined benefit retirement plans moving toward larger exposure to emerging markets


More than half of investment consultants said they are likely to increase public defined benefit (DB) plans’ exposure to emerging market equities and 60% expect to boost allocation to emerging market debt over the next 12 to 24 months, according to new research from global analytics firm Cerulli Associates."Emerging and frontier markets are among the least efficient and have the most dynamic and fastest-growing economies worldwide, presenting opportunity in both fixed-income and equity markets for investors,» states Michele Giuditta, associate director at Cerulli. «For these reasons, institutions are including these assets in their portfolios as sources of growth, income, and diversification.»
