Skagen launches new concentrated global equity fund


Skagen launched on Tuesday Skagen Focus – its first new equity fund launched in three years.Skagen Focus will be managed by Filip Weintraub together with Jonas Edholm. Both have long experience as investors and Filip Weintraub was previously a portfolio manager with Skagen from 2001 until 2010.The fund will be an actively managed fund that will invest in undervalued, under-researched and unpopular companies with the potential to increase in value. The fund’s broad and global mandate will enable the managers to search for companies with the best possible risk-adjusted return potential all over the world, regardless of sector, geography or market capitalisation. The fund’s portfolio will be extremely concentrated, comprised of around 35 select core holdings. Top ten holdings will account for around 40-50% of the fund.
