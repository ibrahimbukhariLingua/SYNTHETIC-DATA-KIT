iShares offers four new ETFs on the Swiss market


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The ETF provider iShares has listed four new products on SIX. They are two sustainable funds, iShares sustainable MSCI Emerging Markets SRI UCITS ETF (SUSM) and iShares Sustainable MSCI USA SRI UCITS ETF (SUAS), and the funds iShares Fallen Angels High Yield Corporate Bond UCITS ETF (WING) and iShares US Mortgage Backed Securities UCITS ETF (IMBS), a statement released on 22 August says.
