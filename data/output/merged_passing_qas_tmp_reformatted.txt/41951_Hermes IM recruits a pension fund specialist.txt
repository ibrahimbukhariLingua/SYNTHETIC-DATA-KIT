Hermes IM recruits a pension fund specialist


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The British asset management firm Hermes Investment Management in early November recruited Will Pomroy to the position of manager in charge of socially responsible investment. Pomroy joins from the British National Association of Pension Funds, where he served for over three years as public policy manager, responsible for public affairs, corporate governance and sustainable development subjects.
