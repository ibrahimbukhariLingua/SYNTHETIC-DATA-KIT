Indosuez Wealth Management builds presence in Switzerland


Indosuez Wealth Management on 4 October announced the appointment, effective immediately, of Pierre-Emile Schifferli as head of relations with financial intermediaries, institutional clients and commercial private banking. He joins the board of directors at the wealth management firm of the Crédit Agricole group, the parent company of Indosuez Wealth Management.Schifferli, who reports directly to Patrick Ramsey, head of wealth management, takes over as director of a new “professional client” division, which has 30 employees and is active both in Switzerland and Asia. CA Indosuez (Suisse) had 1,352 employees in 2016, and managed nearly CHF40bn.
