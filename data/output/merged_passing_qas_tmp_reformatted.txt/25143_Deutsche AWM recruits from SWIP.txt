Deutsche AWM recruits from SWIP


Deutsche Asset & Wealth Management has announced the recruitment of Michael Lee as director head of consultants UK, Financial News reports. Lee had for two and a half years been head of consultant relationships at Scottish Widows Investment Partnership (SWIP). Francis Ghiloni, director of distribution and client management, will be taking over his responsibilities at Scottish Widows IP. Before working at SWIP, Lee spent four years at BlackRock as director of consultant relations, and then two years as product manager at SEI Investments.
