Appointment at Credit Suisse


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Credit Suisse has announced the appointment of Marc-Olivier Tschabold as chief investment officer for commercial real estate in Germany. Tschabold replaces Francisca Farina Fischer, who will now concentrate on the management of the Credit Suisse Global Commercial Real Estate Fund. Tschabold had been working in real estate management at Credit Suisse since 2008. Credit Suisse has also announced the acquisition of a property in Dusseldorf for a total of EUR45m. The commercial real estate portfolio now includes properteies in Frankfurt, Stuttgart, Hamburg, Cologne, Berlin, Leipzig and Dusseldorf.
