Schroders welcomes a new director


The British asset management firm Schroders on Wednesday, 7 December announced that Ian King, the current CEO of BAE Systems, will be joining its board of directors as non-executive director from 1 January. King will also chair the nominations committee. King, 60, has served as CEO of BAE Systems since September 2008. Before that, he was appointed as a member of the board of directors at the British company in 2007 as chief operating offier of the group. He also served until 2014 as non-executive director of the Rotork company, a specialist in the manufacture of electric, pneumatic and hydraulic motorisation equipment.
