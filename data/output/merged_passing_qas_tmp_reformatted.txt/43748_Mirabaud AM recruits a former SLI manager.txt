Mirabaud AM recruits a former SLI manager


Mirabaud Asset Management has hired Ken Nicholson who will be joining the firm in September to manage European small and mid cap equity portfolios. Nicholson joins from Standard Life Investments where he was investment director for smaller companies from 2001, and went on to manage the Standard Life European Smaller Companies fund between 2007 and 2014.
