Catella launches a real estate platform in Norway


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Catella is launching a real estate activity in Norway, by acquiring the companies of Jarle Norman-Hansen and Bjarte Solheim, two real estate specialists. The first is the founder and managing director of Ambolt Advisors, a mezzanine fund company based in Luxembourg which manages two funds which finance Scandinavian real estate investment. The second is the founder of the Norwegian asset management firm Elementum. The structure created from these will concentrate on real estate investment and asset management on behalf of Norwegian and international investors. It will also manage mezzanine debt funds. The activity will begin in 2016. Catella has assets under management of EUR14bn, with the real estate management activity representing EUR3.2bn.
