Head of wealth management for Europe at Barclays exits


Francesco Grosoli, head of wealth management activities in Europe, the Middle East and Africa (EMEA) at Barclays, is leaving the firm after 12 years at the group, the specialist website finews reports. Grosoli will be temporarily replaced by Jean-Christophe Gerars, chief investment officer at the Barclays private bank, who joined the bank about 17 months after a 25-year career at HSBC.
