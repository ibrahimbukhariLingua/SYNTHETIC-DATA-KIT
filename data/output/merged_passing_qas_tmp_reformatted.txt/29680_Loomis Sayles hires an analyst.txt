Loomis Sayles hires an analyst


Loomis, Sayles & Company announced today that Bianca Taylor has joined as a senior sovereign analyst primarily responsible for covering Latin America. Bianca replaces Eddy Sternberg, who became an emerging markets portfolio manager last year. She will report to Teri Mason, director of macro strategies.Bianca was most recently with Roubini Global Economics, but began her career as a sovereign bond and currency analyst for Latin America, first with Franklin Templeton Investments and then with Pantera Capital Management LP.Loomis Sayles manages more than $191 billion in assets as of March 31, 2013.
