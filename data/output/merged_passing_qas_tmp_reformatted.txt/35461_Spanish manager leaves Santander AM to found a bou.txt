Spanish manager leaves Santander AM to found a boutique


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The equity manager Ivan Martin Aranguez has left Santander Asset Management to start up a personal project, Citywire reports. The head of Spanish equities, who joined Santander AM in late 2012, has decided to found his own boutique, which will be based in Madrid and which will have the British hedge fund manager Crispin Odey and her husband, Nicholas Pease, former CEO of JO Hambro Capital Management, on its board of advisers. It is planning to launch two funds, which will have about EUR100m in assets.
