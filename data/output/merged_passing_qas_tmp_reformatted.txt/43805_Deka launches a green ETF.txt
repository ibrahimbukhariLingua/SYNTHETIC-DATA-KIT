Deka launches a green ETF


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The German firm Deka has launched an ETF which aims to track a selection of European companies which rank among the best performance in terms of respect for environmental, social and governance (ESG) criteria. The Deka Oekom Euro Nachhaltigkeit UCITS ETF (ticker ELFB) is listed on the Frankfurt and Stuttgart stock exchanges, and charges an annual commission of 0.40%. To evaluate the quality of the implementation of ESG criteria. The fund will be based on the Solactive Eurozone Sustainability Index, which includes 30 top companies with good ratings form oekom research.
