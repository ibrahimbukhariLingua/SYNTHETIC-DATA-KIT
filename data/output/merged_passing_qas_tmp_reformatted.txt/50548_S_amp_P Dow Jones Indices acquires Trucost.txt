S&amp;P Dow Jones Indices acquires Trucost


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The international index provider S&P Dow Jones Indices on 3 October announced the acquisition of Trucost, an independent research agency specialised in environmental data and analysis. The operation, which is effective from 1 October 2016, was completed via the affiliate S&P Global Indices UK Limited. This acquisition will complement the S&P Global Indices and S&P Global ESG (environmental, social, governance) solutions. It also renders it possible to improve “the existing expertise at Trucost, including feeds of environmental data via the EBoard platform,” S&P Dow Jones Indices says in a statement.
