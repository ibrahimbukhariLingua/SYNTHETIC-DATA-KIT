Beamonte Investments launches a venture capital fund for Mexico and Colombia


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Beamonte Investments, a US investment company based in Boston, on 6 January 2016 announced the launch of the Venture Academy Fund, a venture capital fund targeting investment opportunities in Mexico and Colombia. The new investment vehicle will be directed by Enrique Rodriguez, a former investment banker of Grupo Bursatil Mexico. Rodriguez has solid experience in structuring funds, debt issuing, IPOs and mergers and acquisitions. The Venture Academy Fund will target to raise between USD10m to USD15m. It will make investments between USD500,000 to USD1.5m per operation. It will primarily target startups with strong growth and businesses able to seize market opportunities via online platforms, mobile applications and other technologies.
