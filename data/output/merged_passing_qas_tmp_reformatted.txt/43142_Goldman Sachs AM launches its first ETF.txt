Goldman Sachs AM launches its first ETF


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Goldman Sachs Asset Management on 21 Septemnber announced the launch of its first ETF, the ActiveBeta® US Large Cap Equity ETF (Ticker:GSLC), the first in a series of ETFs which will replicate the house index, the Goldman Sachs ActiveBeta® index, a methodology which aims to outperform the market. The new ETF, which initially has USD50m in institutional assets, has fees of 9 basis points, the same commission charged for the largest ETF in terms of assets, the SPDR S&P 500 ETF Trust (SPY). Other ETFs derived from the ActiveBeta methodology are expected to be launched in the next few months. As of the end of June 2015, assets under supervision by Goldman Sachs Asset Management totalled USD1.020trn.
