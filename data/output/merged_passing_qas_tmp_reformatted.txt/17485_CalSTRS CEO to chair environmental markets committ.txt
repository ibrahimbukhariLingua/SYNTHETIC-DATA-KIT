CalSTRS CEO to chair environmental markets committee of FTSE


FTSE Group announced that Jack Ehnes, CEO of CalSTRS (California State Teachersâ€™ Retirement System) will serve as the new chairman of the FTSE Environmental Markets Committee. The FTSE Environmental Markets Committee is the independent advisory body responsible for setting the rules governing the classification system and the two sets of indices based on it, the FTSE Environmental Technology and FTSE Environmental Opportunities indices. Jack Ehnes replaces Winston Hickox, who is stepping down after five years chairing the Environmental Markets Committee, which is comprised of investment industry practitioners.
