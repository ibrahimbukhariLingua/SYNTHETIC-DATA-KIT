Amundi adds to its ETF range dedicated to corporate bonds


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Amundi ETF is adds to its product range dedicated to corporate bonds with the launch of a new share class in the Amundi ETF Floating Rate USD Corporate Ucits ETF, entitled Amundi ETF Floating Rate USD Corporate Ucits ETF - Hedged EUR. The share class is hedged against dollar/euro currency risks. It aims to replicate the performance of the Markit iBoxx USD Liquid FRN Investment Grade Corporates 100 index as closely as possible. The product charges 0.20%. “In a market context characterised by very low interest rates and expectations of a rate hike, variable rate bonds may interest investors seeking to reduce their exposure to interest rate movements: they are characterised by low sensitivity to fluctuations in interest rates, since their coupons follow the evolution of rates. Particuarly, their potential returns move in the same direction as the movement of interest rates,” Amundi ETF says in a statement.
