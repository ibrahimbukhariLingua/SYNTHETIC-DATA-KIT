Lazard AM Deutschland poaches distribution head from Metzler AM


Lazard Asset Management Deutschland has announced the appointment of Daniela Weissenborn as head of wholesale distribution for Germany and Austria. Weissenborn, who has 20 years of experience in finance, had previously worked at Metzler Asset Management, where she had been responsible for banks, wealth managers, and family offices in Europe. She has also worked at Threadneedle Portfolio Services, Allianz Global Investors and Commerzbank.
