DeAWM scales up in Italy


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Deutsche Asset & Wealth Management (DeAWM), the asset management division of the Deutsche Bank group, is scaling up its Italian wealth management team, with the recruitment of Federico Scrocco in the position of head of the key clients unit, Bluerating reports. Scrocco, who joins from Merrill Lynch, will report to Luca Caramaschi, head of wealth management in Italy for DeAWM. Scrocco, who has been in the position since 1 August, will aim to develop the activities of the firm which provides wealth advising services to high net worth private clients from seven offices located in Italy. It will concentrate on clients who have assets of over EUR70m.
