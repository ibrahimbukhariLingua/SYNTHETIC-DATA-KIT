Mitsui &amp; Co acquires 20% of a boutique dedicated to infrastructure debt


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The Japanese financial group Mitsui & Co has signed an agreement with Westbourne Credit Management Limited, an Australian boutique specialised in the management of infrastructure debt funds, with the objective of acquiring 20% of its capital. Through this investment, “Mitsui will become the first Japanese firm to participate in specialised fund management activity for infrastructure debt,” the Japanese group says in a statement. The size of the operation has not been disclosed.
