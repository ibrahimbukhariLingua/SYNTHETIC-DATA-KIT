BNY Mellon IM reveals new name of North American affiliate


Following the merger of its North American boutiques, the BNY Mellon Investment Management group (BNY Mellon IM) on 27 September announced that the new body will be entitled Mellon Corporation from 2 January 2019. The structure was constructed over the course of 2018, by combining active equity strategies from the Boston Company, active bond strategies from Standish Mellon, and index-based, multi-factor and multi-asset class strategies from Mellon Capital. These activities had previously been united under the name BNY Asset Management North America Corporation.
