Heirs of Albert Frère take helm at GBL


Following the death of Albert Frère on 3 December, his children are inheriting his investment holding company. The investment holding firm GBL announced that his successor had been appointed. His son Gérald Frère, and his daughter, Ségolène Gallienne, both directors, will control a majority of capital in the Frère – Bourgeois company. Along with the Desmarais family and Pargesa Netherlands, they control 51.63% of GBL. Frère, 67, had been chairman of the board of directors of the holding company since 2012. Gallienne, 41, a director since 2015, is married to Ian Gallienne, who exercises operational control of GBL with Gérard Lamarche.
