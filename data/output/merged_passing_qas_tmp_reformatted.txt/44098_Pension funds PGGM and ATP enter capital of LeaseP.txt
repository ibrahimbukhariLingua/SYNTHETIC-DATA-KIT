Pension funds PGGM and ATP enter capital of LeasePlan


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The Netherlands pension fund PGGM and the Danish pension fund ATP have acquired a minority stake in the capital of the Nethlerlands automotive fleet management firm LeasePlan. 100% of the latter firm has been acquired by a consortium of international investors for USD3.7bn. In addition to PGGM and ATP, tbe consortium of investors includes the Abu Dhabi sovereign fund ADIA and the Singapore fund GIC, the investment fund TDR Capital, and the Merchant Banking division of Goldman Sachs.
