Deutsche Bank targets “moderately” wealthy clients


In an interview with Wirtschaftwoche, Pierre de Weck, head of the wealth management division of the group, says that Deutsche Bank has gained significant market share in Germany in the high net worth sector recently.Deutsche Bank is now planning to concentrate its efforts in investors whose wealth comes to EUR1m to EUR10m.To that end, the bank will recruit advisers, and is planning to open new dedicated branches. The same applies to its affiliate, Sal. Oppenheim.
