Robeco recruits from DWS


Robeco on 26 February announced the appointment of Alexander Preininger to the newly-created position of head of institutional clients for the Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA) region. In this new role, Preininger will work with local offices in the EMEA region to meet growing demand on the part of clients for broader partnerships and cooperation between regions. Preininger will report to Christoph von Reiche, head of global distribution and marketing and member of the executive board. Preininger had previously worked at DWS, the asset management affiliate of Deutsche Bank, where he had been head of institutional coverage in the EMEA region and advising to managing directors at DWS International.
