Syz Asset Management attacks German market


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The Swiss asset management firm Syz Asset Management is taking its first steps into the German market with the opening of an office in Munich this month, the specialist website finews reports. Initially, the Munich-based affiliate will be led by the distribution specialist Michael Schlieper, who will concentrate on institutional clients in Germany as well as in Austria. The largest markets for Syz AAM, with assets under management totalling about CHF17bn, have hitherto been, in order, Italy, the United Kingdom, France, the Benelux countries, and Spain.
