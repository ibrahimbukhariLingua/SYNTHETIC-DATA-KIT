Spain - Managers reduce their exposure to equities


According to the latest monthly statistics from Lipper, the top Spanish management firms are planning to reduce exposure to equities in their portfolios and to increase the proportion of lower-risk assets in March. 61% of managers surveyed say that they are underweight in equities, and only 7.69% say they are overweight, Cinco DÃ­as says.
