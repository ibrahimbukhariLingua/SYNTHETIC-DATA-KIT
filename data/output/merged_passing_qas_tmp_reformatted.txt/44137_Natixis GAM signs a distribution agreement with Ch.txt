Natixis GAM signs a distribution agreement with CheBanca!


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Natixis Global Asset Management (NGAM) has signed a distribution agreement with CheBanca!, the retail online bank of the Italian Mediobanca group, according to several Italian media sources. Funds from NGAM have been added to the already vast range of investment products on sale to clients in Italy from CheBanca! via its dedicated savings platform. Under the agreement, CheBanca! will offer its clients the full range of funds on sale from NGAM in Italy, including expertise from Loomis Sayles in bonds, the range from Natixis Asset Management, and Seyond products.
