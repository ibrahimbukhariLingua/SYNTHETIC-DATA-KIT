Mirabaud AM recruits institutional sales chief in Zurich


Mirabaud Asset Management has recruited Stefan Stucki as its senior head of institutional sales for the German-speaking Swiss market. Stucki had previously worked for Fisch Asset Management, where he had been director of sales, senior partner, and a member of the executive board, since 2007. Stucki previously served at Credit Suisse, and has over 30 years of experience in financial services, and has served in positions related to structuring of products, customer services, relationship management, and sales.
