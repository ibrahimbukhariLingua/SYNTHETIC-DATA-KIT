Financière de l’Echiquier hires a manager from Exane Derivatives


La Financière de l’Echiquier (LFDE) has hired Emmanuel Kragen as asset allocation manager, a newly created role. He will report to Marc Craquelin, head of asset management. Kragen moved from Exane Derivatives where he has been working as global economist and strategist within the asset allocation team since 2008.
