BlackRock teams up with British firm Tavistock Investments


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The British financial services group Tavistock Investments on Monday, 28 September announced that it has signed a cooperation agreement between its affiliate Tavistock Wealth and the US asset management firm BlackRock. The objective is to be able to offer a new portfolio range, entitled “Tavistock Profiles,” aimed at customers and financial advisers of Tavistock. The new range will combine the ACUMEN passive portfolios from Tavistock, including ETFs, with the actively-managed range from BlackRock.
