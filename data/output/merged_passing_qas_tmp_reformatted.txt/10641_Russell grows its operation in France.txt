Russell grows its operation in France


Russell Investments has started in 2011 with 4 people in Paris. It will have 12 by Q1, 2012. The company will not only sell asset management, but also smart execution. And it bets on two strategies: high dividends, with the Global High Dividend fund (USD150m, managed by Thornburg) and listed infrastructure (a new fund has already USD100m under management).The Paris branch manages at present some EUR3bn for French customers, of which EUR1bn for institutional investors and EUR2bn in the frame of partnerships with banks and insurance companies.
