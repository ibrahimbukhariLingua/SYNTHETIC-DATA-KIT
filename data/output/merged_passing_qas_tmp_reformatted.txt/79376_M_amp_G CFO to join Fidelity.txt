M&amp;G CFO to join Fidelity


Grant Speirs, chief financial officer at M&G for over 10 years, has left the firm to join Fidelity International, Financial News has learnt. He joins Anne Richards, former CEO of M&G, who left the firm to take over at Fidelity last year. A spokesperson for M&G has confirmed the departure, and a spokesperson for Fidelity states that Speirs will join the firmmid-March.
