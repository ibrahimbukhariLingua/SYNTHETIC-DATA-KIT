Amundi ETF posts inflows of over EUR3.4bn in 2015


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } In 2015, Amundi ETF has posted net subscriptions of over EUR3.4bn, bringing assets to over EUR20bn. Inflows were primarily to European equities and euro bond segments which offer potential for returns. “Allowing our clients to access new sources of potential returns and to develop Smart Beta solutions are at the core of 2016,” says Valérie Baudson, CEO of Amundi ETF, Indexing & Smart Beta, in a statement. The head also says that she is ready to export the expertise of Amundi ETF to Asia in 2016, playing on the strengths of the asset management firm, “based on competitiveness of costs, quality of replication and innovation.”
