Amundi recruits a head of ETFs for the United Kingdom and Ireland


Amundi has recruited Ashley Fagan as head of development for ETFs, trackers and smart beta in the United Kingdom and Ireland, and global head for coordination of key contracts for ETFs, trackers and smart beta. Fagan joins from BlackRock, where she worked for 10 years, most recently as head of iShares for institutionals in the United Kingdom and Ireland.
