SEB Wealth Management posts strong inflows for 2014


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } In 2014, SEB Wealth Management, the division of the Swedish bank dedicated to asset management, recorded net inflows of SEK90.2bn, or EUR9.7bn, of which SEK54.8bn were from institutionals, and SEK35.4bn from the private bank. These inflows brought assets in the division to SEK1.635trn, which represents about EUR176bn, 65% of which are managed for institutional clients, and 35% for private banking. At the end of 2013, assets totalled SEK1.408trn, or EUR151bn.
