American Century Investments recruits a manager


Tsuyoshi Ozaki, Ph.D. joined American Century Investments July 31 as a portfolio manager within its Disciplined Equity group. He co-manages the U.S. Disciplined Large Cap Growth (Disciplined Growth/NT Disciplined Growth), U.S. Disciplined Large Cap Long/Short (AC Alternatives Disciplined Long Short), and U.S. Utilities (Utilities) strategies, reporting to Vice President and Portfolio Manager Yulin Long, Ph.D., CFA.Ozaki, who has worked in the investment industry for more than 18 years, joined American Century from Axioma Inc., where he served as director and senior member of the Global Equity Analytics team, supporting the quantitative and portfolio analytic requirements of large, sophisticated quantitative asset managers and hedge funds.
