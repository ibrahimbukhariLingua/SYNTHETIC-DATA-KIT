Groupama AM offers small and midcap expertise to foreign investors


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The asset management firm Groupama Asset Management on 17 December announced that it will now be offering international investors access to its expertise in the management of European small and midcaps. Its expertise Groupama Avenir Euro, previously available as a French-registered fund, has now been inserted into the Luxembourg Sicav G Fund Euro Small and Mid Cap. The new sub-fund is a feeder fund of Groupama Avenir Euro, the French asset management firm says. The objective for the fund is to generate higher returns than the MSCI EMU Small Cap index (net dividends reinvested).
