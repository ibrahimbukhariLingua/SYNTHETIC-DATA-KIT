VanEck doubles size of ETF product range listed in Milan


VanEck is launching nine Think Core ETF products on Borsa Italiana, bringing the number of its ETF products listed in Milan to 18, Funds People Italia reports. The new ETFs offer access to sustainable equities, global real estate securities, equities and global and European bonds, and to high-dividend companies.
