Crux AM launches a concentrated fund of British stocks


@page { size: 8.27in 11.69in; margin: 0.79in }p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 115%; background: transparent }Crux Asset Management, the asset management firm founded by Richard Pease, will on 17 September launch the FP Crux UK Special Situations fund, a British equity fund managed by Richard Penny, who joined the firm in June 2018 from Legal & General Investment Management.The new fund will invest in a concentrated portfolio of British companies, with potential exposure to other developed markets. The fund will focus on small and midcaps which have been neglected and which Penny considers attractive. The manager will invest a part of his wealth in the fund, to ensure alignment of interests with investors.
