Aon Hewitt adds to hedge fund research team


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The consulting firm Aon Hewitt is adding to its hedge fund research team, with the recruitment of Alison Trusty as hedge fund analysy on its research team dedicated to liquid alternative strategy managers. Trusty will concentrate on event-driven strategies, credit strategies, and funds of hedge funds. In her new role, Trusty will identify, evaluate, undertake due diligence on and monitor hedge funds worldwide. She will also develop training programmes for the various strategies used by hedge funds. Trusty most recently worked for five and a half years at Hymans Robertson, where she was in charge of resarch and advising to hedge funds and hedge fund managers.
