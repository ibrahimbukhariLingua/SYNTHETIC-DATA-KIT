Assets under management and profits up for BCV group


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; }a:link { } Assets under management the Banque cantonale vaudoise (BCV) rose 3%, or CHF2.5bn, in first half, to a total of CHF86.3bn, according to a staement released on 21 August. Net inflows in the half totalled CHF51bn. Funds on the Swiss market posted inflows of CHF546m, but offshore funds at the same time saw net redemptions totalling CHF495m. The BCV group has also reported an increase of 2% in its net profits, to CHF154m. The cost/income ratio has improved to 60% from 61% previously.
