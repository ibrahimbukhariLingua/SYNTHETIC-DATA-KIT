Credit Suisse AM closes its sales office in Spain


Funds People reports that Pedro Domenech has left Credit Suisse Asset Management (CSAM), and that the management firm has decided to close its sales office in Madrid, and will transfer responsibility for the Spanish market to Andrea Sanguinetto, head of the CSAM distribution team in Milan. Sales efforts in Spain will now focus on alternative products and core products such as bonds and multi-asset class products.
