Santander sells 50% stake in asset management arm


The private equity investors Warburg Pincus and General Atlantic will acquire 50% of the holding company for the asset management arm of Santander, Santander Asset Management (EUR152bn), for slightly over EUR1.023bn, which corresponds to nearly 1.35% of assets.
