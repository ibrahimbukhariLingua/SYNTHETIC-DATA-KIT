Fidelity to advise Ashburton for multi-asset funds


AshburtonInvestments has enrolled Fidelity International to assist them to improve and advise on its multi-asset activities, citywire reports. The South African firm is retaining responsibility for strategic asset allocation, while Fidelity will contribute and help to manage tactical asset allocation. This concerns both securities and funds. The agreement covers international multi-asset fund activities for EUR85bn at Ashburton.
