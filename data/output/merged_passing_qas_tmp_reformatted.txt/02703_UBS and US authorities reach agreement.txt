UBS and US authorities reach agreement


UBS, Switzerland and the US tax authorities have reached an out-of-court settlement of litigation related to a tax evasion scandal in which the Swiss bank was suspected of complicity, L’Agefi reports. “The US tax authorities will drop their charges against the Swiss bank, which is suspected of complicity in tax evasion, when the final agreement is signed,” the newspaper reports. It remains to be seen what US clients of UBS will decide.
