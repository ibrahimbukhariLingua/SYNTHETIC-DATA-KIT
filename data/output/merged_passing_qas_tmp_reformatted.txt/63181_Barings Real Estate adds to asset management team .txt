Barings Real Estate adds to asset management team in Spain


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Barings Real Estate Advisors has recruited Carlos de Oya as director of its asset management activity for Spain, as part of a strategy to develop its European offerings. Oya will report to Adolfo Favieres, country head for Spain. Oya, who has over 25 years of professional experience, joins from Redevco, a commercial real estate investment management firm, where he had served as a portfolio and investment manager. Before that, he served at Knight Frank and Cushman & Wakefield.
