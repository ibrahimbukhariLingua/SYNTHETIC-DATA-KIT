Amundi appoints a new CEO for Italy


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Amundi has appointed Alessandro Varaldo as its new managing director and CEO for Amundi SGR, its Italian affiliate. Varaldo had previously worked at the Intesa Sanpaolo group, where he was head of sales for Eurizon Capital, responsible for products, advising, distribution and marketing, and then head of savings products, investment and retirement planning at Banca dei Territori. He has also been a member of the board of directors at Centrovita and Penghua, a Chinese asset management firm. Varaldo has also worked at Finanza e Futuro, Fideuram Asset Management, Sanpolo Invest Ireland AM and Capitalia.
