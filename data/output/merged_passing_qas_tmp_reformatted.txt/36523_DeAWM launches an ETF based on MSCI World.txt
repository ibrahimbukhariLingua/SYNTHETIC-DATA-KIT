DeAWM launches an ETF based on MSCI World


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Deutsche Asset & Wealth Management (DeAWM) has launched a new physical replication ETF in its db X-trackers Core range. The ETF, listed in London, replicates the performance of the MSCI World index, which covers the largest equity markets, a statement says. It charges 0.19%. A listing on the Frankfurt stock exchange is planned in August.
