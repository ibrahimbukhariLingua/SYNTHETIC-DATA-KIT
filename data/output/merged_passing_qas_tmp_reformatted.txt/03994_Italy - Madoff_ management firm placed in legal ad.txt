Italy - Madoff: management firm placed in legal administration


The chairman of the Italian regulator Consob, following a consultation with the Bank of Italy, has suspended the administrative organs of the Italian management firm Independent Global Managers Sgr. for 60 days, and appointed Angelo Pappadà as legal administrator in charge of the management of the firm’s affairs.The measures were taken pursuant to article 53 of het Testo Unico della Finanza, relating to serious violations of asset management regulations, according to a statement from Consob.The Italian press is reporting that the decision is related to investments by Independent Global Managers in the activities of Bernard Madoff. The investments are reported to total EUR30m, or nearly the company’s entire portfolio.
