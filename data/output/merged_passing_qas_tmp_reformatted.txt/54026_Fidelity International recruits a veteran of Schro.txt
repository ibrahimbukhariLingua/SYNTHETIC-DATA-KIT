Fidelity International recruits a veteran of Schroders in Italy


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Fidelity International has recruited Donatella Principe as head of fund selection unit and international sales in Italy. Principe had worked at Schroders Italy SIM since 2002, and had most recently served as chair of the investment board and head of the institutional activity. Principe will report to Francesca Martignoni, country head for Italy at Fidelity International.
