Warburg Invest Luxembourg rebrands to Augeo Capital Management


The Luxembourg-based asset management firm Warburg Invest Luxembourg, owned by the Apex group, has changed its name to Augeo Capital Management, with effect from 1 February 2019, the firm has announced on its website. The new name is derived from the latin augeo, meaning to increase, grow, enlarge. The firm was founded in 1989, and has a full asset management license. Its list of funds includes both special funds dedicated to institutional investors, and mutual funds on a white label platform dedicated to traditional and alternative fund managers and advisory firms.
