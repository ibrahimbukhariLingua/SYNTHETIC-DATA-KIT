Sycomore extends offerings in German market


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 115%; background: transparent none repeat scroll 0% 0%; } The Sycomore Eco Solutions fund is now available to German investors, the specialist website Das Investment reports. Sycomore Asset Management launched the themed fund in 2015. Sycomore Eco Solutions concentrates on actors whose business models contribute to the ecological and energy transition in five areas: mobility, energy, renovation/cnstruction, circular economy, and activities related to ecosystems. It excludes businesses whose activities are wholly or partly destructive of natural capital, or whose ESG rating is inadequate.
