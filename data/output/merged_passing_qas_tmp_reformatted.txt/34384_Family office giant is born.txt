Family office giant is born


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } According to Investment Week, the two British family offices Fleming Family & Partners and Stonehage are merging, to create Stonehage Fleming Family & Partners. The new entity, which will be the largest family office in the Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA) region, with GBP27bn in assets under management, will be led by Giuseppe Ciucci, current CEO of Stonehage, while the CEO of FF&P, Adam Fleming, will be a board member.
