CIFC closes a CLO with USD559.9m


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; }a:link { } CIFC Corp has closed a collateralised loan obligation (CLO), CIFC Funding 2014-V, LTD, representing USD559.9m in total and about USD550m in new assets under management. The transaction was steered by RBC Capital Markets and carried out with the support of 14 institutional investors, including newcomers to CIFC. Since the beginning of the year, CIFC has sponsored five CLI transactions, representing USD3.3bn in new assets under management.
