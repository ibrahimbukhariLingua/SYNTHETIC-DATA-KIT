SEB shakes up its fund selection activities


SEB Asset Management is planning to centralise its multi-management activity in Stockholm, following the departure of its fund-picking team based in Frankfurt, Citywire Global reports. Two members of the team have already left. The third, Andreas Bichler, is also expected to leave the firm at the beginning of January, the website states.
