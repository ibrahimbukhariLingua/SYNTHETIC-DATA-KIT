Axa IM and HEC Paris launch a MOOC of asset management


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Axa Investment Managers and HEC Paris are launching a massive open online course (MOOC) dedicated to asset management. The MOOC, entitled Investment Management in an Evolving and Volatile Woeld, aims to provide better understanding of the investment universe and its impact on each of us. The training course is available free to all on the online platform Coursera. It is divided into four modules: how to define investment objectives? How to design an investment solution? What are the specific techniques of portfolio management? How to select a fund manager or practise this profession? It is possible to take the online course free of charge, or to take a paying option (USD70) which grants a certification. The MOOC is available in English, subtitled in French and Chinese.
