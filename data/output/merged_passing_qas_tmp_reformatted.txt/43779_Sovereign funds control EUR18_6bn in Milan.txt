Sovereign funds control EUR18.6bn in Milan


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The sovereign funds of the world control EUR18.6bn in equities on the Milan stock exchange, according to calculations by Il Sole 24 Ore, based on data from S&P Capital IQ, reported by Bluerating. This represents an increase of EUR6.5bn, or 56%, over the levels at the start of the year. This is due both to a rising stock market and to the entrance of new players, such as China.
