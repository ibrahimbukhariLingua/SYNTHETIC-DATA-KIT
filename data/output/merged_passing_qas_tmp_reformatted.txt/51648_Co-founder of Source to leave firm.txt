Co-founder of Source to leave firm


Peter Thompson, co-founder and president of the European exchange-traded fund (ETF) provider Source, will be leaving the firm by the end of 2016, eFinancialNews reports. Thompson recently took on the interim position of CEO, following the departure of Ted Hood, the other co-founder of the firm. As of 30 June 2016, Source had USD20.5bn in assets under management, and 3.9% market share in the ETF sector, according to data from the firm ETFGI. When approached by eFinancialNews, a spokesperson for Source stated that no decision had yet been taken about a potential replacement for Thompson.
