Departure of a small cap manager from Artisan Partners AM


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The US asset management firm Artisan Partners, which has USD109.5bn in assets under management, has announced the departure of David Geisler, co-manager of the Artisan Global Small Cap Fund. According to Citywire, Geisler had worked at the asset management firm based in San Francisco for seven years. The vehicle is now co-managed by Mark Yockey and Charles-Henri Hamker, a role which they have occupied since the launch of the fund in June 2013.
