Spanish funds posted inflows of EUR1.2bn in August


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } In August, Spanish common investment funds posted net inflows of EUR1.22bn, after EUR3bn in July, according to provisional statistics released by Inverco, the Spanish asset management association. Since the beginning of 2016, net subscriptions have totalled EUR6.16bn. As of 31 August 2016, assets under management in the industry totalled EUR224.7bn, up by EUR1.6bn, or 0.7% compared with the end of July 2016. Since 2016, assets under management have risen 2.2%, or EUR4.85bn.
