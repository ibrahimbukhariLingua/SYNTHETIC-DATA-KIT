EFGAM recruits a head for activities in Singapore


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } EFG Asset Management has recruited Rebekah Chuan as CEO and chief investment officer at EFGAM Singapore. Chuan joins from DBS, where she spent five years building discreationary portfolio management. Before that, she worked for 10 years at HSBC (Suisse) Private Bank and in Singapore. At EFGAM, she will manage activities for the Singapore region. The recruitment is as part of development projects for EFGAM in Asia.
