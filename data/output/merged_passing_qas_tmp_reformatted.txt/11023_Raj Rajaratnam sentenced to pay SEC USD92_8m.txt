Raj Rajaratnam sentenced to pay SEC USD92.8m


US judge Jed Rakoll has ordered Raj Rajaratnam, founder of Galleon, to pay a fine of USD92.8m to settle accusations of insider trading by the Securities and Exchange Commission, the Financial Times reports. The fine is three times larger than the illegal profits that Rajaratnam made, and comes in addition to USD64.8m that he is already required to pay. He was also sentenced to 11 years in prison.
