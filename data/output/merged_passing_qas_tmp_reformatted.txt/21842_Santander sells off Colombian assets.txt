Santander sells off Colombian assets


The Chilean bank Corpbanca SA on 30 May bought a 51% stake in Banco Santander Colombia SA and Santander Investment Trust Colombia, for USD624m, Investment Europe reports. The remaining shares and the Colombian affiliates of Santander will be acquired by 30 June.
