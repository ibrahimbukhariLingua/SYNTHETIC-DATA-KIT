NN IP signs distribution agreement with BPM


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } NN Investment Partners has signed an agreement with Banca Popolare di Milano (BPM) by which the range of bond, equity and diversified funds from the Netherlands-based asset management firm will be available to wealth management clients of the Italian bank through its private banking activity. The Milan-based bank offers a total of over 3,500 funds.
