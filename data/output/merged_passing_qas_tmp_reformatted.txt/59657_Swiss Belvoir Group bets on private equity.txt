Swiss Belvoir Group bets on private equity


The young investment boutique Belvoir Group, created in autumn 2016, has developed its private equity activities. With this in mind, the firm, which already has 35 employees, is seeking to recruit 10 sales associates, Daniel Fasnacht, CEO for the firm since July, tells the specialist website finews. Belvoir group is also seeking to recruit an additional employee for client management.In an environment in which Swiss private banks and asset management firms are developing or scaling up their private equity activities, Belvoir Group is planning to take advantage of the current fashion for alternative sources of returns to bet on direct investment in innovative Swiss companies.
