DeAWM emphasizes digital


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Deutsche Asset & Wealth Management (DeAWM) is recruiting for its Digital Business unit, with the appointment of Kai Bald as Head of Digital Distribution. He will be responsible for digital strategy at the asset management firm, particularly relationships with online fund distribution platforms and Internet wealth management firms. Another objective for Bald will also be to identify new solutions to strengthen product sales online. Bald previously served as head of passively-managed retail funds at DeAWM.
