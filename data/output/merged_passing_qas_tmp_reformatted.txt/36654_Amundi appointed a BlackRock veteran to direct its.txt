Amundi appointed a BlackRock veteran to direct its Japanese team


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; }a:link { } Amundi has recruited the former director and chief investment officer (CIO) from BlackRock Japan, Yasunori Iwanaga, as its Cio for Japan, Asia Asset Management reports. Hiwanaga replaces Futoshi Saito. Iwanaga, who joined Amundi early in July, will lead a team of 50 people in Tokyo. He joins from the asset management firm Incapture Japan, where he worked at a consultant from 2013 to 2014. Before that, he worked for 13 years as CIO at the Japanese office of BlackRock. Iwanaga previously worked for NCB Investment Management and Nippon Credit Bank.
