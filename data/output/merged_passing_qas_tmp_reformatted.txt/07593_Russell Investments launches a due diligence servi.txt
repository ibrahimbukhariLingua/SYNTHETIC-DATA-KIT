Russell Investments launches a due diligence service


Russell Investments has announced the launch of the Manager Compliance & Operations Review (Manager CORE) service, aimed at institutional investors. The service will allow them to “understand and manage business, operational and compliance risks related to their outsourced management contracts, through improved knowledge of the managers,” a statement says. Institutional investors who subscribe to the Manager CORE service will have access to data provided to Russell Investments by more than 60 regional regulatory agencies, including from emerging markets.
