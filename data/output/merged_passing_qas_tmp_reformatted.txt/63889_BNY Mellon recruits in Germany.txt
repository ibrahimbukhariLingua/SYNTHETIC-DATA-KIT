BNY Mellon recruits in Germany


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } BNY Mellon Investment Management is continuing its drive on the German market, with the recruitment of Christian D’Amico as head of instituitonal and wholesale sales, and who also joins the German distribution team, based in Frankfurt and led by Thilo Wolf, country head for Germany, and Silvia Mischel, head of institutional sales. D’Amico, who had worked for over 10 years in distribution, had most recently been head of relationships with instituitional invetors in Germany and Austria at Janus Capital. BNY Mellon Investment Management, which in a statement emphasizes its desire to grow on the German market, opened its first office in Germany in Frankfurt in July 2015.
