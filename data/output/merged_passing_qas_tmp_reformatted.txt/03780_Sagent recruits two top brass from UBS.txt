Sagent recruits two top brass from UBS


Sagent Advisors has announced that it has recruited Anthony Martino and Gregg Newman as co-heads of its alternative capital markets division. They will be in charge of structuring and placing new products, and providing advice to businesses on a vast range of capital structuring questions. The two new recruits worked together previously to direct the entire capital markets group at UBS; they will be based in New York.
