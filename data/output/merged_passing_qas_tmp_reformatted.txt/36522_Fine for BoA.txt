Fine for BoA


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } According to Agefi, Bank of America was yesterday sentenced by a New York court to pay a fine of USD1.27bn for abusive sale of mortgages from Countrywide to Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac. Bloomberg reports that the US bank may this week announce an out-of-court settlement with the Department of Justice of the thorny sub-prime credit issue. BoA may agree to pay USD13m to USD17m.
