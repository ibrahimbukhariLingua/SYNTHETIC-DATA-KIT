Indosuez Wealth Management Italia recruits a Deutsche Bank veteran


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 115%; } Indosuez Wealth Management Italia has recruited Maria Pia D’Angella as senior wealth manager, responsible for development of wealth management activities, who will report to Luca Caramaschi, CEO of Indosuez Wealth Management Italia. D’Angella joins from Deutsche Bank Spa, where she had been senior wealth manaager and head of the team in the Asset & Wealth Management division since 2006.
