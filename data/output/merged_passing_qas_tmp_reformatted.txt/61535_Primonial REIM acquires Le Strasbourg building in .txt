Primonial REIM acquires Le Strasbourg building in Paris


Primonial REIM, the real estate asset management affiliate of the Primonial group, on 3 May announced that it has acquired the “Le Strasbourg” office building, located in the 10th district of Paris, from Eurosic. The total amount of the investment is about EUR79.3m. The property was acquired on behalf of the SCPI Primopierre. The property, renovated in 2006, has a useful floor area of about 9,000 square metres, and 75 parking spaces. It is wholly leased to two tenants, La Poste on the ground floor of the building, and SNCF in the upper, office part of the structure, Primonial REIM states.
