Jupiter veteran launches his own company


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } Daniel Frauenfelder, who had previously worked for Jupiter Asset Management, has decided to go it alone, and has founded the company Triple S, which begins its activities at the beginning of September. The new firm, based in Zurich, is specialised in the distribution of sustainable investment for third-party institutional investor partners in Switzerland, Liechtenstein and Austria.
