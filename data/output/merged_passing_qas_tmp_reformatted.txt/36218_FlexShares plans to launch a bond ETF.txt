FlexShares plans to launch a bond ETF


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } The ETF provider FlexShares, an affiliate of Northern Trust, is planning to launch a corporate bond ETF based on corporate fundamentals, the specialist website ETF.com reports. Offerings are limited in this segment, whose most eminent representative is the PowerShares Fundamental High Yield Corporate Bond Portfolio (PHB B-70), based on an index from Research Affiliates, with over USD570bn in assets under management. The new ETF from FlexShares is expected to redistribute the weightings of the components of the house index Northern Trust U.S. Investment-Grade Corporate Bond, according to liquidity and the fundamentals of issuers.
