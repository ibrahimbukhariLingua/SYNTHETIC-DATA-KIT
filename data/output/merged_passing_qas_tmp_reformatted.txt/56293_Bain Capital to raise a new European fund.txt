Bain Capital to raise a new European fund


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Bain Capital is seeking to raise up to EUR4.5bn for its new European fund, Private Equity News report, citing a source. This is much more than the EUR3.5bn the firm collected for its last European vehicle in 2014. Another source indicates that the fund may aim for about EUR3.5bn, but may raise more, depending on demand.
