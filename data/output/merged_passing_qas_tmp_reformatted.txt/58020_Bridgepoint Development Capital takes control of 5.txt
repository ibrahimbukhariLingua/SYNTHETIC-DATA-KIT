Bridgepoint Development Capital takes control of 5àsec


Bridgepoint Development Capital, the smidcap activity of Bridgepoint, yesterday announced that it is reshuffling its capital and taking control of 5àsec, a specialist in textile cleaning and maintenance. The Capzanine, IFE Mezzanine and Quilvest Private Equity funds are selling their entire stakes. Parquest Capital and Rive Private Investment will retain minority stakes. 5àsec, founded in Marseille in 1968, is now present in 31 countries. In 2016, the group had earnings of nearly EUR85m, via its network of 1,730 stores (branches and franchises).
