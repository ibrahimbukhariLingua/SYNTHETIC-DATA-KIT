Rothschild recruits a non-executive chairman for south-east Asia


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } The Rothschild group has recruited Henry Ho (Ho Hen Cheong) as non-executive chairman of its south-east Asian activities. Ho joins the Rothschild office in Singapore, and will work in close collaboration with financial advising and wealth management groups worldwide, to continue and accelerate the development of Rothschild activities throughout the region. Ho has long worked with the Singapore sovereign fund Temasek, for which he served as managing director from 2009 to 2010, and was the CEO of one of the companies in its portfolio, PT Bank Danamon Indonesia, from 2010 to 2015. Before that, he worked at Citibank, where he served in a variety of positions of responsibility in Singapore, Malaysia and Thailand. He has also served in senior positions at PT Bank International Indonesia, from 2004 to 2009, and at Saudi American Bank in Saudi Arabia.
