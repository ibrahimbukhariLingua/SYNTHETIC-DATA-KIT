BMO recruits a head of sales in Italy


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; } BMO Global Asset Management has recruited Alfredo Uras as a salesperson for Italy. Uras joins from Pioneer Investments, where he spent more than ten years. At BMO, he will report to Giampaolo Giannello, head of sales for Italy, and will be based in Milan.
