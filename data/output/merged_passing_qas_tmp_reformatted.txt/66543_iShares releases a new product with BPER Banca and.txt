iShares releases a new product with BPER Banca and Arca Vita


iShares, an affiliate of the BlackRock group, has extended its partnership with the Italian bank BPER Banca, and the Italian insurance company Arca Vita, with the launch of a new ETF insurance solution developed by Arca Vita, which will be distributed via branches of the BPER group, Bluerating reports.
