Sycomore AM to market thematic funds in Spain


French SRI boutique Sycomore Asset Management has announcedthe registration of two thematic funds in Spain, Sycomore Happy@Work and Sycomore Eco Solutions. The firm had already made a pair of funds - Sycomore Sélection Responsable and Sycomore Shared Growth - available to Spanish investors.Sycomore Happy@Work invests in companies displaying high scores in Sycomore’s human capital assessment while Sycomore Eco Solutions invests in companies that contribute positively to the energy transition. Both funds have a three-year track-record. In addition to the registration of the funds, Sycomore Asset Management will join SpainSIF, the Spanish association for sustainable investment.
