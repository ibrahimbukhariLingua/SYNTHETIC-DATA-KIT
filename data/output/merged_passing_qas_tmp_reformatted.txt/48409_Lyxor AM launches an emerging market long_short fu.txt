Lyxor AM launches an emerging market long/short fund with Capricorn


p { margin-bottom: 0.1in; line-height: 120%; } Lyxor Asset Management and Capricorn have launched a long/short fund based on global emerging markets, Investment Week reports. The Lyxor/Capricorn GEM Strategy UCITS relies on a strategy launched by the South Africa-based asset management firm. It becomes the fifth alternative asset management firm to team up with Lyxor for its UCITS platform.
