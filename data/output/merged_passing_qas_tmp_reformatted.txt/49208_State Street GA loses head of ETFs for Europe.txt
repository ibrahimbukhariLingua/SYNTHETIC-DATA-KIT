State Street GA loses head of ETFs for Europe


p { margin-bottom: 0.25cm; line-height: 120%; }a:link { } Alexis Marinof, previously head of SPDR ETF for Europe, the Middle East and Africa (EMEA), the affiliate of State Street Global Advisors (SSGA) dedicated to ETFs, left the US asset management firm in November. “After 13 wonderful years at State Street Global Advisors, I am very excited to be looking for a new challenge,” Marinof writes on his profile page. Marinof, based in London, had been head of SPRD ETF for the EMEA region since April 2013. Before that, he served as chief operating officer in charge of distribution for the EMEA region at SSGA. At the US asset management firm, he previously held the successive positions of head of Scandinavian countries, then head of Middle East and Africa. Before joining SSGA in 2003, Marinof had worked at J.P. Morgan, in the asset management division.
