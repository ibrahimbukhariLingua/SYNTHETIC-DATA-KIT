DNCA recruits an equity analyst from BNP Paribas AM


DNCA Finance has recruited Yingying Wo, who is joining the Quality Growth team, with a total of EUR500m in assets under management. She will serve as Senior Equity Analyst, and will work alongside Carl Aufret and Rajesh Varna. Before joining DNCA Finance, Wu belonged to the teams at BNP Paribas Asset Management in Paris, where she was co-manager of global mandates, and covered the discretionary consumer product sector. DNCA, now part of Natixis GAM, has EUR16bn in assets under management as of 20/04/2015.
