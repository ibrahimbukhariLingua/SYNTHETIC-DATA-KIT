Fullerton recruits a new CEO


The asset management firm Fullerton Fund Management, an affiliate of the Singapore sovereign fund Temasek, on 16 October announced the appointment of Jenny Sofian to the position of chief executive officer from 1 November 2017. Sofian joins from Amundi, where she had served as CEO for Singapore and head for South-East Asia. She replaces Ho Tian Yee, chairman of the board of directors at Fullerton, who had since late 2016 served as interim CEO.
